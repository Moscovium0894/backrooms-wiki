Juice is an Item in Escape the Backrooms. It appears to be a Juice in a bottle.

Appearance

Juice is a green liquid stored in a plastic bottle with a blue bottle cap.

Location

This Item can be found in:

  • Overgrowth
  • Level 4 - Abandoned Office
  • Level 5 - Terror Hotel
  • Level Fun
  • Level 6 - Lights Out
  • Level 7 - Thalassophobia

  • Level 10
  • Level 0.2
  • The Snackrooms
  • Level 188
  • Level Fun+
  • Level 52
  • Level 55.1

Use

Upon using, the player will take off the cap and drink the Juice. Then, you'll receive a Speed Boost for a temporary amount of time (10 seconds), and your Sanity will go up by 25%. During the time of the Speed Boost, your Stamina is not drained.

Speed Boost

In some Levels, the Speed Boost is slightly different. Because Juice sets your Speed to a specific value (rather than increasing it by a percentage), the Speed Boost lasts longer or shorter depending on what Level or location you're in.

  • 50%: Level 52
  • 35%: Level 3, Level 37, Level 6, Level 37.2
  • 22.7%: Level 0, The Hub, Level 4, Level 5, Level 7, Level 8, Level 0.11, Level 9, Abandoned Outpost, Level 3999, Level 0.1, The Snackrooms, Level !~!, Level 188, Level 55.1
  • 12.5%: Level 1, Level 2, Level Fun, Level !, The End, Level 9223372036854775807, Level 94, Level 10, Level Fun+

Trivia

  • The liquid in the bottle moves around with the player's movements.
  • Overgrowth is the only secret level where Juice can be found, likely due to the fact that it's the only secret level that drains Sanity.
