Knifes are an Item in Escape the Backrooms. It appears to be a knife.

Appearance

Knifes resemble a metal combat knife with a wooden handle. It can be used to cut down ropes and Vines tangling players.

Location

This Item can be found in:

  • The Grassrooms

Use

Using the Item

Upon using, the player swings the Knife.

Purpose

Cutting the Ropes

If a swung Knife interacts with 3 different ropes around The Grassrooms, the ropes will break, allowing progression of the level.

Vine-Free Knifes

If you are playing with 2 or more people and a player gets stuck in Vines, it will take a few seconds before they die which gives other players time to use a Knife on them which will cut down the Vines, allowing the trapped player to become free.

Teammate Death

If you're playing with 2 or more people, the Knife can kill other players if they are hit by it, which will cause them to fall over and remain lifeless on the ground.

Trivia

  • A player's hazmat suit after getting killed by a Knife will become a ragdoll after a short death animation where they fall down.
  • In Fancy's 11th Devlog, he showed a player killing a player stuck in Vines with the Knife. Despite the fact that you can kill other players with a Knife, this would've freed the player from the Vines in-game.
