LiDAR Scanners are an Item in Escape the Backrooms. It appears to be a scanner.

Appearance

LiDAR Scanners are a yellowish/orange scanner with a screen connected to it, which shows a radar tracker. On the side of the Scanner, there is a keypad and a fingerprint scanner underneath. The keypad and fingerprint scanner currently have no use.

Location

This Item can be found in:

  • Level 6 - Lights Out

Use

Using the Item

Upon using, a bunch of dots get placed on walls for a few seconds, which only works in the 2 mazes of Level 6.

Purpose

Multiple Dot Colors

The dots that come out of the Scanner can be a bunch of different colors, and the colors change whenever you stop holding the Scanner and hold it again.

Tracker

When you're holding the Scanner, you can see the screen that shows the radar tracker. Every couple seconds, light will go around the area on the screen, and the Wretch in Level 6 will be shown as a red dot if it is within the area. Other players can be seen as green dots.

Trivia

  • There is no limit to how many dots can be on the walls at once.
  • The LiDAR Scanner is the only Item to not have an updated icon after Version 1.0.
    • The reason it doesn't have an updated icon is unknown.
  • The LiDAR Scanner was added so Level 6 isn't just bumping into walls in a pitch-black level until you find the exit.
