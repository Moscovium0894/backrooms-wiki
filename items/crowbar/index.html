Crowbars are an Item in Escape the Backrooms. It appears to be a silver stick.

Appearance

Crowbars are rusty, red-colored sticks, bent on its sides. The top and bottom of the Crowbar has a gray coloring instead of red.

Location

This Item can be found in:

  • Level 4 - Abandoned Office
  • Abandoned Outpost

Use

Using the Item

Upon using, the Crowbar is swung by the player.

Purpose

If the Crowbar is nearby any breakable Vent, the Vent will break open, allowing players to crouch and enter it.
