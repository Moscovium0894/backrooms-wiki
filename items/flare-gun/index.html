Flare Guns are an Item in Escape the Backrooms. It appears to be a orange gun.

Appearance

Flare Guns are orange guns that shoots flares to clear out fog.

Location

This Item can be found in:

  • Level 7 - Thalassophobia

Use

Using the Item

Upon using, a Flare is shot out of the gun.

Purpose

The purpose of Flare Guns is for 2 or more players so you can let your teammates know where you're at using one, since the Flare shot out of the gun can be seen in the fog.

Trivia

  • When The Flare Gun was first shown in Fancy's 6th Devlog, it had a Rarity of Epic, however this was changed for unknown reasons.
