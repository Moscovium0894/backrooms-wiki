Glowsticks are an Item in Escape the Backrooms. It appears to be a tube.

Appearance

Glowsticks are plastic tubes that glow different colors. They can be found in 4 different colors: Green, Red, Blue, and Yellow.

Location

This Item can be found in:

  • Level Fun+

Use

Using the Item

Upon using, the player drops the Glowstick on the ground.

Purpose

In the Arcade Sector of Level Fun+, different symbols can be found in the dark zone. If you are holding a certain Glowstick in front of each one, the respective symbol will light up. If you place down all of the correct colors in front of all 4 of the symbols, the locked door in the Sector will open.

Trivia

  • Glowsticks are the only Item in the game with variations.
  • Glowsticks can be found at the ditch in the final area of Level 8 with the color Orange, however they cannot be picked up.
  • If you drop all 4 Glowstick Colors in the same spot, they will glow up as white.
  • Glowsticks were first seen in Part 2, when they were found underneath the Tables in the locked door room at the end of Level Fun.
