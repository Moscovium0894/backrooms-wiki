Flashlights are an Item in Escape the Backrooms. It appears to simply be a Flashlight.

Appearance

Flashlights are 1990's to 2010-sized flashlights. It can be used to see in Dark Areas and to stun Hounds or make Smilers disappear.

Use

Using the Item

Upon using, the player turns on the Flashlight and a Light is shined through the Flashlight.

Purpose

The main purpose of Flashlights is to allow players to see in dark areas using the light, but a Flashlight can also be used to stun Hounds and keep them in place, and to make Smilers in Level 0.2 and Level 55.1 disappear.
