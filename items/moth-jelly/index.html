Moth Jelly is an Item in Escape the Backrooms. It appears to be rectangular piece of purple jelly.

Appearance

Moth jelly is a shiny purple rectangle made of Jelly, and it closely resembles grape Jelly. It can only be obtained by using Bug Spray on Male Deathmoths.

Location

This Item can be found in:

  • Level 5 - Terror Hotel
  • Level 9 - Darkened Suburbs

Use

Upon using, the player eats the Moth Jelly. After this, you receive a Speed Boost for a temporary amount of time. Your Sanity and Stamina will also be restored.

Speed Boost

Moth Jelly provides the player with the benefits of an Energy Bar and Juice combined, meaning an increase in Speed for 10 seconds, fully restoring your Stamina and granting an additional, temporary 12.5 seconds of Stamina that will not regenerate, but is only used while the player is Running (the Stamina will start regenerating once it reaches the Base Stamina threshold for that specific Level). Additionally, Moth Jelly restores 35% Sanity upon consumption.

Trivia

  • Moth Jelly is the rarest edible Item in the game, despite Liquid Pain having a rarity of Legendary.
    • This is because Liquid Pain can be found in 5 levels while Moth Jelly can only be found in 2.
  • In the original Backrooms lore, Moth Jelly is produced by Female Deathmoths, instead of Male Deathmoths.
