Spray Paint is an Upcoming Item in Escape the Backrooms. It appears to be a spray can.

Appearance

Spray Paint resembles Bug Spray but with text on the back and it has the ability to Spray Paint arrows on the wall.

Location

This Item is planned to be found in:

  • Level 0.1

Use

From what has been seen of Spray Paint, upon using, a preview of an Arrow will appear on the wall and can be moved anywhere. Upon clicking again, an Arrow will be formed.

Trivia

  • The reason this Item is being added is to have a way to find your Items if you die.
