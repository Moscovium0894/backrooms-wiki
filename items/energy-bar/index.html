Energy Bars are an Item in Escape the Backrooms. It appears to be a chocolate bar.

Appearance

Energy Bars are chocolate bars that give one energy. The wrapper is half red and half brown and says, "Dark Chocolate Cacao", seemingly being the flavor of the bar. It also says that there is a 35g source of protein in the bar. On the inside is a chocolate bar with little bits of chocolate.

Use

Upon using, the player opens up the Energy Bar and eats it. After consumption, your Sanity is restored by 25%. You'll also receive a Stamina Boost.

Stamina Boost

The Stamina boost allows the player to run for a total of 12.5 seconds and only goes away as the player uses their Stamina through running. Extra Stamina does not regenerate back (Extra stamina = stamina over the default stamina threshold. For example, in The Hub your default stamina threshold is 4 seconds, meaning you can sprint for a total of 4 seconds before running out of breath. Once you eat an energy bar in The Hub, your stamina will increase to 12.5 seconds, however, it will not regenerate until it is below 4 seconds. For information on the stamina threshold of every level, see Base Stamina) Energy Bars do not stack, which means the maximum number of stamina you can have at a time is 12.5 seconds. In addition, an Energy Bar replenishes 25% Sanity.

The Player's Base Stamina is slightly different depending on the Level they are on. The recovery speed after completely running out of Stamina is always 7 seconds, but the regeneration speed is sometimes different, allowing you to run more if you wait for your Stamina to regenerate without allowing it to completely deplete.

  • Infinite: Level !~!
  • ~7.5 seconds: Level 9223372036854775807, Level Fun+ (sectors)
  • ~5.5 seconds: Level 3
  • ~4.5 seconds: Level 0, Level 2, Level 5, Level Fun, Level 37 Level ! , The End, Level 94, Level 6, Level 7, Level 8, Level 0.11 Level 9, Abandoned Outpost, Level 10, Level 3999, Level 0.1, The Snackrooms, Level 188, Level 37.2 , Level Fun+, Level 52, Level 55.1
  • ~4 seconds: The Hub, Level 4
  • ~3.5 seconds: Level 1

This only applies to the main areas in the listed Levels. Some Levels like Level Fun+ have other areas that have different values. Level Fun+ in this case has your base Stamina last for ~7.5 seconds when you're in one of the Sectors. More cases like this might be present but not tested.

Trivia

  • Overgrowth is the only secret level where Energy Bars can be found, likely due to the fact that it's the only secret level that drains Sanity.
  • Energy Bars grant stamina upon consumption, which might be a bit hard to tell since there's no stamina bar or indication (excluding the item description and the name energy bar) that it gives you a stamina boost or the stamina boost was given.
  • The flavor of the Energy Bar "Dark Chocolate Cacao" mentions cacao (also known as a cocoa bean or cocoa) in the name, which is the thing that chocolate comes from.
