Threat assessment
Partygoer Dangerous

Partygoers are yellow humanoid creatures with blood being easily noticeable on the mouth, hands, and legs. They hold Balloons and they stay in the same position, but if they spot you they will start chasing you. In Level Fun+ , they walk around the place and don't just stand in one place.

FIELD TIP — Crouch when trying to get around one.

Party Host Indirectly Deadly

Party Hosts are cakes with bright green icing with an Eyeball in the middle. The color of the eye is blue. Party Hosts are immobile and will always be on the wall or the ceiling.

FIELD TIP — Throw a Firework directly at them from a safe distance to kill them and not kill yourself from the blast.

Appearance

Level Fun is a children's birthday party with orange walls and fuzzy blue carpets. The level also has Tables to hide under. This level is the home of the Partygoers.

Objective

Part 1: The First Half

Once you first spawn in, you're going to want to travel until you find a Table blocking the path. Crouch underneath this Table to get through it, and on the other side there is another Table. Pop the Balloons on this Table to attract the nearby Partygoer's attention and then hide underneath it. This should turn the Partygoer around that was previously looking straight at the pathway. Crouch your way past these nearby Partygoers until arriving at the hallway at the end. In the next room, you're going to want to head down the straightforward hallway and crouch behind a turned around Partygoer and stay next to the wall of the open area. Just don't go right, because that area is a trap, and don't go left before heading down the straightforward hallway.

Part 2: The Second Half

Upon getting to the next hallway, you'll come across a larger area with a lot of Partygoers around. Once you get into the room, head straight up from the hallway and at the end of the hallway, go right. You'll need to alert the Partygoers and go down the nearby hallway, which has a Table in it. Hide underneath the Table and head back out into the hallway. This time, run down the hallway where the Partygoers see you and go through the left hallway once reaching the end. Keep running down the hallway until you see a room on the right with 2 Tables. Hide underneath one. In the next area, you're going to want to crouch next to the right wall to avoid a Partygoer and get to the next hallway.

Part 3: The Party

This hallway connects to a room that looks completely different from the rest of the level. It appears much more colorful and has some Tables with items. There are no Partygoers here either. To escape the level, find the hallway with white tiles that's located in this room, and it will lead to a poolroom. The poolroom has 4 tube-shaped Slides that lead to the next level. Go down one (doesn't matter which one) and you'll arrive at Level 37.

Release Version

This level was one that was added on the game's Release. The level layout is the same as it is now except there wasn't the room that teaches you to sneak by the Partygoers. The Partygoers also had their original model. The other difference was the party room was an arcade room and the Level 37 entrance was completely different. The arcade room had the wall texture of the room that contains the blue key in Level Fun+. The arcade room had some arcade machines that Partygoers were playing, 2 long Tables, and Partygoers that were watching the Table on the left. You would have to quickly run into the Table before the Partygoers reached you and then go to the other side. Once on the other side, you would have to get out of the Table and quickly run over to the one on the right and then get to the middle of that Table. Then, you would have to make a run for the open hallway that has the normal Level Fun orange walls and get chased by the Partygoers. After running down the hallway for a bit, you would arrive at the exit room and the Partygoers wouldn't be able to enter. There was an LED Light, and all you had to do was touch it to be instantly sent to Level 37.

Trivia

  • The photo for the level is slightly outdated because the balloons still have older textures.
  • There is a locked blue door at the end of the level that cannot be opened without the UUU Client. Behind it is a very dark room which contains two rows of Tables and Glowsticks sitting under them. This room was the old Level Fun arcade room that the level ended with in the game's Release. Part 2 changed this arcade room and the arcade room was stored behind the blue door. Currently, this room has no use, however this may change in the future.
  • The party room is reused from a found footage on YouTube.
  • The Release level selector description mentions a host, which may seem like a mention of Party Hosts, however this is unlikely considering the time between when Level Fun and Party Hosts were added to the game, and how Party Hosts are found in a way further level than Level Fun.
  • There is a Note that was found in the game files when Part 2 released that seemed like it would have been used in Level Fun. This Note was originally unused but returned in Level Fun+.
  • In Fancy's 1st Devlog, he said that he modeled this level after a place he used to go to. It is unknown what this place is for certain.
  • Fancy's goal with the Release version of Level Fun was basically to make it extremely hard, however this caused a lot of negative reception towards the level, so it was changed to how it is in Part 2.
