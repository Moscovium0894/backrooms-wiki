Appearance

Level 7 is an ocean that seems to stretch on for miles. There are houses all around the ocean and one that has a lighthouse on top of it and a Shark Tank connected to it. The ocean has sand across the floor, and rocks all over the place.

Objective

Part 1: Follow the Light

When you first spawn in, you'll be in a regular house with some items everywhere, and the downstairs of the house is flooded with dirty water. A window downstairs is your only way out of this house, which leads to the rest of the ocean. There are 4 Boats around the house. Get in a Boat and start traveling. Go from house to house and get some Items the houses have to offer. One of the houses at the beginning has some Flare Guns inside and a Note next to them. Use them to your advantage! At a certain point in the ocean, fog will appear and the lighthouse at the final house will activate after a few minutes. Follow the light in order to get to the final house. This house has a Shark Tank connected to it and four Diving Helmets next to it. Put on a Diving Helmet and enter the Tank.

Part 2: Find the Cave

Once in the Tank, you will go down into the ocean. When going down, The Thing on Level 7 will try to get you, and it ends up breaking the Tank forcing you to swim around the bottom of the ocean. When swimming around, look for an entrance to a Cave inside of a rock structure. There are lights inside, so you know that it's where you need to go. Enter the Cave and follow the path to arrive at Level 8.

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Find the door in Level 6 to get here.

Exits

  • Go upstairs in the first house to find a door that leads to The Hub.
  • Find the Cave entrance when underwater to get to Level 8.

The Hub Door

To find The Hub Door in this level, you first enter the starting house, go through the door to get upstairs, and at the top of the stairs will be the door.

Trivia

  • This was the last level in the game in Part 2.
    • Once getting downstairs in the starting house, there would be a boat at the front door, and the player would have to touch it. Then, a cutscene would play of the player rowing away in the boat. Then, the credits would roll.
  • At one point, it was possible to see the box and loading zone for The Hub out a window on the first floor of the house. The Hub teleport now unloads from the game when the player walks through the doorway at the very end of the staircase in the starting house (proven with the UUU Client).
  • This Level came as a surprise to most players since it was never mentioned or shown in Fancy's videos until after Part 2 released and Fancy showed this Level as a sneak peek for a new Level in Part 3 before Part 2 released on his Discord Server.
  • In Fancy's first Devlog, he said that making the Backrooms levels in order would get repetitive and a lot of the levels are pretty boring, mentioning Level 6, Level 7, and Level 10. Ironically though, all three of these levels have still ended up in the game.
  • When this level was added in Part 2, there were only 3 houses.
  • The Note on this level shows a drawing of the original model of The Thing on Level 7.
