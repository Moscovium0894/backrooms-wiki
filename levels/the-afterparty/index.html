Partygoer Dangerous

Partygoers are yellow humanoid creatures with blood being easily noticeable on the mouth, hands, and legs. They hold Balloons and they stay in the same position, but if they spot you they will start chasing you. In Level Fun+ , they walk around the place and don't just stand in one place.

FIELD TIP — Crouch when trying to get around one.

Partypooper Friendly

Partypoopers are friendly humanoid entities that wear black jeans, a black hoodie, and black gloves. They also wear a blue mask with a sad face on it, but their actual face can never be seen by normal means. Majority of Partypoopers are dead after the events of The Fun War and only one is still seen alive in-game.

FIELD TIP — Follow the Partypooper's directions.

Trivia

The level

  • Out of all the upcoming levels, this one has the most amount of information about it, having some information revealed about it and a look at two rooms.
    • The other upcoming levels include the Unknown Dark Level as the one with the second most amount of information about it, only having a blurred picture, and Level ??? as the one with the least amount of information about it, because it's not even known for sure what the level is and was only mentioned.
  • In Fancy's 13th Escape the Backrooms devlog, it was said that the level is a class variable danger level, which means the actual danger of the level is uncertain. No level in the game so far has ever been a class variable, however.

Production facts

  • The highlighted wiki text when Fancy explained this level in his 13th Escape the Backrooms devlog reads, "The Afterparty appears to be an abandoned series of party rooms. Popped balloons, deflated balloons, unpopped balloons, and birthday cakes can be found all around the level.". What Fancy said is, "which appears to be an abandoned series of party rooms with balloons and birthday cakes found all around.".
  • In Fancy's 13th Escape the Backrooms devlog, Fancy described the party rooms as "super liminal rooms with tables that kind of remind you of the ones you'd see at a party".

Lore & theories

  • In Fancy's 13th Escape the Backrooms devlog, Fancy said the lore of this level is that "after the Partygoers won the Fun War, they threw the craziest party in Backrooms history" and that "word around the Backrooms is that the Partygoers really know how to have a good time.". Right after this in that same devlog, a montage plays of the Partygoers partying in the Level Fun+ arcade sector, Level 52 and The Afterparty while the song "BACK2BACK" plays.

Level inspiration

  • This level is based on the Backrooms level "The Afterparty".
