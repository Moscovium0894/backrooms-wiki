Threat assessment
Partygoer Dangerous

Partygoers are yellow humanoid creatures with blood being easily noticeable on the mouth, hands, and legs. They hold Balloons and they stay in the same position, but if they spot you they will start chasing you. In Level Fun+ , they walk around the place and don't just stand in one place.

FIELD TIP — Crouch when trying to get around one.

Partypooper Friendly

Partypoopers are friendly humanoid entities that wear black jeans, a black hoodie, and black gloves. They also wear a blue mask with a sad face on it, but their actual face can never be seen by normal means. Majority of Partypoopers are dead after the events of The Fun War and only one is still seen alive in-game.

FIELD TIP — Follow the Partypooper's directions.

Appearance

Level 52 is a school infested with Partygoers, and also where The Fun War happened. Near the beginning of the Level, there are some dead Partypoopers, with graffiti on the wall saying, "WE WON THE WAR". Despite Partypoopers seemingly having gone extinct due to The Fun War, 1 Partypooper is still alive in this Level.

Objective

Part 1: Find the Partypooper

Once you first spawn in, you will be in the starting class, which is a room with pillars and desks that contain items. There are also doors that lead to the rest of the level. Proceed through these doors and go through the hallway.

Part 2: Follow the Partypooper

Shortly into the Level, you will find a Partypooper. Once getting close to him, he will start moving. Follow the Partypooper, as he will guide you to the exit. However, if he crouches, you must crouch as well because it means that there is a Partygoer nearby. If he is standing up, that means it's safe. If you don't crouch when the Partypooper crouches, the Partygoers will come after you. After a short while of travelling through the school with the Partypooper, you will arrive at the exit. The Partypooper will wave, and now you must go up the stairs to get to Level 55.1.

Entities

Only 2 entities can be found in this level. These entities are:

  • Partygoer (15)
  • Partypooper (1 alive, 4 dead)

Items

Items you can find in this level are only at the very start and are on the desks. These items are:

  • Almond Water
  • Juice
  • Energy Bar
  • Flashlight
  • Liquid Pain

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Open the gate in Level Fun+ and enter it to get here.

Exits

  • Follow the Partypooper until he arrives at the exit. Then, go up the stairs and you will get to Level 55.1.
  • Go through The Hub Door at the start of the level to get to The Hub.

The Hub Door

To find The Hub Door in this level, in the starting class, go to the right side of the room. On the right side of the room is the Door.
