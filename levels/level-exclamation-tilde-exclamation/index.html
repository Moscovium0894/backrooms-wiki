Threat assessment
Clump Dangerous

Clumps are large, fleshy beasts that appear to have tons of human arms on their bodies. They have a torso solely made out of their mouth, and a tail on the back of them. Their mouths have round teeth and a large tongue, and they devour their victims upon finding them.

FIELD TIP — Run away from it in the red hallways.

Smiler Moderate

Smilers are floating smiling faces with sharp teeth with dark shadowy fog surrounding their bodies. Smilers are mysterious and shadow-like entities that are typically seen in pitch black places where only their smiling faces can be seen.

FIELD TIP — Try to stay in the light.

Appearance

Level !~! is a hotel with red lights similar to Level !. The Level consists of many hallways with a carpet, wooden doors and many exit signs hanging from the ceiling. There are also some blue hallways, which appear to be hallways that are blue, and act as safe zones from the Clump that chases you in this Level.

Objective

Part 1: Finding the Clump

Once you first spawn in, you will notice a gate blocking off the rest of the Level. In the left hallway lies a Pressure Plate, and in the right hallway lies another Gate. Stand on the Pressure Plate, and the gate in the right hallway will open. Proceed down the hallway and after a right turn will be a Lever on the left hall and forward is a dark hallway. Pull the lever, and a Clump will come out of the darkness.

Part 2: RUN

Turn around and the gate that blocked off the rest of the Level before will be open. Enter the hallway and keep running. Doors on the left and right will start opening, so make sure to avoid those. As you're running, you'll eventually arrive at a big room with 2 hallways and a table in the center. The left hallway is a blue hallway, and the right one is a red one. If you enter the red hallway, a Smiler will come after you if you go too far down. Enter the blue hallway, and the Clump will enter the red hallway. In the blue hallway, there is a Chainsaw on a table, so make sure to pick it up. Go through the door in the blue hallway, and on the other side of it is a hole. Jump down, and you'll fall down the hole. Once you fall down, immediately turn around because you'll be facing an open hallway that the Clump will be in. On the other side is a boarded-up door. Use the Chainsaw on the door and make sure to be quick because the Clump will be right behind you. After 2 uses of the Chainsaw, the door will be openable. Go through the door and you'll arrive at a big room with balconies. Go through the left or right side and get to the door on the other side, however you could also jump across the table in the middle. Keep running until you arrive at a small room with tables with chairs on them.

Go around the tables and enter the next hallway. The next room you'll arrive at is a kitchen with a hole in the ground. A counter is in the middle of the room, so make sure to go around it on the right. Go through the hole the arrow points at. You will then fall through a blue hallway and instantly be back at the red ones. On the right hallway is the Clump, so you will have to go forward. Keep going until you arrive at a hallway that splits off into 2. The left direction is the way to go. You will then arrive at a room with an object blocking the way. Go around it and go left. The next room is a staircase that goes down for a while. Go down the stairs until you spot an open door. You can keep going down the stairs after that, but you will go the wrong way. Enter the open door and go right in it. You will then spot a hallway that goes up and goes left, but there's also a hallway that is lower and goes right. If you take the right hallway, another Smiler will come after you if you go too far down, and a fake blue hallway can be found. The door to it will close if you try to go near it. The right hallway is the right way to go. Keep going through the right hallway and eventually, you'll find another area that cuts off into 2 hallways. This time, you must go right. Keep going forward in this hallway, and you will find a staircase that goes up to a blue hallway. This is the final blue hallway in the Level, and once you enter it the Clump will leave you alone. Enter the door on the left hallway the blue hallway leads to, and on the other side is Level 188.
