Appearance

The Bunker is a small, abandoned hideout bunker that seems to have been made by M.E.G. It's very small and only contains a few rooms, and the M.E.G. base area has a liminal room behind glass.

Objective

Part 1: Getting into the Bunker

There are multiple ways to enter the level. One is by obtaining an Ending (except for the Final Escape Ending) in which you'll wake up in the bunker room where a hall connects to it and you can use an ID Card to get back to the M.E.G. base area of the level. Another way to get here is by using the Elevator in The Hub's M.E.G. Base which is open if the Wrong Door Ending has been unlocked. Just press the "B" button at the bottom of the floor selection, and you'll be here in no time. The Elevator will bring you to the M.E.G. base area in the level, and in it, you can find 2 pinboards that show the layout of all the levels in the game which also shows where secret levels can be found.

Part 2: Off to Overgrowth

To get to the next level, go to the M.E.G. secured door in the M.E.G. base area and use an ID Card to scan on the card scanner to unlock it. You can access your ID Card by pressing C. The dark hallway on the other side leads to Overgrowth.

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Press the "B" button on the Elevator found in The Hub's M.E.G. Base after getting the Wrong Door Ending to get here.
  • If you get any Ending excluding Final Escape, you'll wake up in the bunker room in this level afterwards.

Exits

  • Take the Elevator out of here, it can bring you to the Ending machine room of Level 3999 or The Hub's M.E.G. Base.
  • Scan an ID Card on the card scanner connected to the M.E.G. secured door in the main area and enter it to get to Overgrowth.

Trivia

  • The Bunker seems to act as a secret hideout hidden below The M.E.G. Base, considering the Elevator and M.E.G. connections.
  • The main level layout pinboard has a couple mistakes:
    • Strangely enough, the positions of Level 37.2 and Level Fun+ are swapped.
    • Level You Cheated is not documented at all on the pinboard, likely to keep it a complete secret.
  • The hallway that connects the bunker room and the M.E.G. base area is the same one from the cutscene seen at the start of Story Mode.
  • Behind one of the glass windows in the hallway that connects the bunker room and the M.E.G. base area are a couple hazmat suits that are used by non-player Wanderers excluding the Skin-Stealer's disguise.
  • The bunker room has 7 bunk beds and 14 individual beds in total.
  • Likely due to this level's size and multiple entrances and exits, the Bunker cannot be accessed from The Hub Mode. Likely due to the same reason, there is no Achievement for completing the level either.
    • Because of this, it seems like it isn't a level at all, but it has been mentioned multiple times that it's a level, such as it being a part of the level layout teaser and the level layout pinboard, and a lot of Version 1.0 promotional material mentioning it to be one of the new levels added in the update.
  • In a video from the Escape the Backrooms TikTok account, it was said that the bunker room in the level would not get good reviews on TripAdvisor, which would make sense considering the condition of the room.
  • In the announcement post for Version 1.0's release date, this level was described with the summary "expand your M.E.G research in the Bunker/Bunker" (Bunker was used in the steam post, and the Bunker was used in the post from the Escape the Backrooms Discord Server).
  • This level and Level You Cheated are currently the only ones Fancy has not talked about the development of in his Devlog series.
  • This level was first mentioned in the announcement for Version 1.0's release date.
  • The exact appearance of this level wasn't known for awhile with one possible appearance being the fourth image from the final page of the Version 1.0 ARG. This appearance was confirmed when a picture of the level was shown by Mattfor on the Discord Server and on the Escape the Backrooms TikTok account.
  • This level might be based on Level 92 from the Backrooms Wikidot since one exit to the level on the Wikidot page calls Level 92 "the Bunker".
    • After the release of Version 1.0, has a more likely chance to be a custom level similar to Level 55.1 since it just seems to be there to explain where the secret levels are located at.
