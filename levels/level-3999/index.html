Appearance

Level 3999 resembles an empty, abandoned and modern arcade. The majority of the level is filled with neon lights and arcade machines lining the walls. The main structure consists of the entrance, the abandoned arcade hall, and the mini arcade.

Objective

Part 1: Get to the Mini Arcade

Once you first spawn in, you will spawn in the entrance. Go through the doors at the start of the Level, and on the other side will be a long, damaged, abandoned and rundown arcade hall.

Part 2: The Mini Arcade

At the end of it is a small room with a bunch of arcade games. There's an Elevator with a code, and the code is somewhere within the room. At the top right corner of the room, there is a table with several Tickets. The Tickets display the number 931. Type in this code on the Elevator and it will activate. On the trip a video from The M.E.G. Civilization will play on a TV suspended in a ceiling corner. It talks about being aware of Noclipping and how the Traffic Lights in front of the Noclipping floors work in Level 0.2. If you don't want to watch it you can press any of the elevator buttons to skip it, but the game doesn't recommend it. The video will not replay in the same save file, so it is not a good idea to skip it. Once the video is done (or you skipped it), the elevator will arrive at Level 0.2.

Level 3999 - The Neon Paradise

Ending Machine Room

Next to the miniature arcade lies a room sealed off by a locked door that contains some arcade machines, some displaying Endings if you have gotten them. You can get here from The M.E.G. Base located in The Hub by pressing the 3999 button. If you've gotten all 4 Endings, you can unlock the main Ending by interacting with the large M.E.G. arcade machine at the top of the room. If done, you'll get the Final Escape Ending.

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Use the guiding lights of the Windmills to guide yourself to the arcade in Level 10.
  • Enter its respective door in the Hub.

Exits

  • Type in the code 931 into the Elevator and watch through the video that plays on the TV (or just skip it) to get to Level 0.2.
  • Enter the staff only door in the abandoned arcade hall to get to The Hub.
  • Enter the revolving door at the entrance to get to Level 10.

The Hub Door

The Hub Door is very easy to find, but possible to miss. In the abandoned arcade hall, on the left side before entering the hall, there will be an open staff only door and behind it is The Hub.

Trivia

  • This was the final level in the game as of Part 3.
    • The hall in the abandoned arcade hall was filled with darkness and upon entering the darkness, a cutscene would play of the lights lighting up and the hallway would go on for a seemingly infinite amount of time. Then, the credits would roll.
  • In Fancy's 8th Devlog, he said that leaving this level will take you to Buffalo, New York in reality, however this didn't seemingly end up happening, however the level was used for the Final Escape Ending, where the player may or may not have ended up to Buffalo, New York, but they certainly do escape the Backrooms alive.
  • Level 3999 is the only level in the game that has a level number with 4 digits.
