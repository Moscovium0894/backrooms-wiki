Threat assessment
Hydrolitis Plague Minimal

The Hydrolitis Plague is a bacteria-like plague that can be found in the Dark-Tiled Rooms in Level 37 and in some of the water in Level 37.2. It is extremely dangerous, so stay away from it, and use the Floatie Boat to get past it. Symptoms of the Hydrolitis Plague include pain and fever and there's no cure for it.

FIELD TIP — Stay in the Floatie Boat if it is in the water of the Expanded Poolrooms .

Clump Dangerous

Clumps are large, fleshy beasts that appear to have tons of human arms on their bodies. They have a torso solely made out of their mouth, and a tail on the back of them. Their mouths have round teeth and a large tongue, and they devour their victims upon finding them.

FIELD TIP — Run away from it in the red hallways.

Appearance

Level 37.2 looks mostly like Level 37 but with new locations, darker areas, and much more danger.

Objective

Part 1: Entering the Expanded Poolrooms

Once you first spawn in, you will find yourself in a big room with windows, and a hole that you must enter to proceed onto the rest of the Level. When you enter the next area, there will be a pool that has the Hydrolitis Plague. Then you will find a Floatie Boat. Get in the Boat and start travelling. Shortly in, the path will have no more water, which means you will have to take a ladder to go up to the Valves. There are 4 Valves, and all you need to do is turn them all on. They are on the right side as well. Once you turn on all the Valves, the path will fill with water, and you will be able to continue on. You will then go down a very fast water path. In the next area, the water will be too low to continue. There will be a nearby ladder, and when you get up, the entrance to the first Dark Poolrooms area will be accessible. There will be 3 ladders, and you will arrive in the first Dark Poolrooms area after going down any one of them.

Part 2: Visiting the Dark Poolrooms

In the Dark Poolrooms, you must go around and turn 4 Valves in order to fill up a tank with water in it. There are also rooms that contain grass, fake outside walls, and giant mushrooms. These rooms resemble Level -33.5 from the Spanish Backrooms Wiki. Each area has one of these rooms. Just make sure you keep an eye out for the Clumps in the Level. If it spots you, just hide in the water and he will leave you alone. Once all of the Valves are turned, you can go back to the Expanded Poolrooms.

Part 3: Back on the Surface

When you get back to the Expanded Poolrooms, the water will be filled, and you will be able to get back in the Floatie Boat and continue on. However, shortly into travelling, you run into another obstacle, which is a giant pit. You must then once again go up the ladder on the left or right and turn all the valves (this time there's 4 on the left and the right) there's also a bridge that will let you easily get to the other side. However, if you run on it, it will break so be careful. Once all the Valves are turned, the pit will be filled, and you can get back in the Floatie Boat and continue travelling. However, soon enough, there will be another obstacle. The path cuts off, so you must get the boat to the other side by lowering the water level. There will be another ladder that you must go up and the entrance to the second Dark Poolrooms area will be there.

Part 4: Back to the Dark Poolrooms

The Second Dark Poolrooms area is similar to the first one, but with a different map layout and with 3 Valves instead of 4. It's the easiest area. However, there is Hydrolitis Plague in some parts of the Level. Turn all 3 valves and leave.

Part 5: Back to the Expanded Poolrooms

After leaving the second Dark Poolrooms area, go through the triangular entrance to get to the next part of The Expanded Poolrooms. There will be a hole, and inside is the Floatie Boat. After travelling for a bit, you will run into yet again another obstacle. The water won't be high enough to proceed. So, you will have to take the nearby ladder, and then you will have to take the stairs and then you will arrive at the above part of this area. In this above part, the entrance to the third Dark Poolrooms area will be there.

Part 6: Down to the Dark Poolrooms Again

You know how it works. Same as last time, but just a different map layout. Except this time there are 7 valves instead of 4. Turn all 7 and leave.

Part 7: Escape!

Once you return to the Expanded Poolrooms, the water will be high enough to continue. At this point, you will no longer need the boat, so just get on the stairs and on the other side is a pool with 3 entrances to a big room. This big room has a bunch of colored slides with a staircase leading up to them. It also has a beach ball, and life savers from the original Level 37. Go up the staircase and at the top is a room with all 4 of the slides. Enter one of the slides (doesn't matter which one) and you will find yourself in Level Fun+.

Entrances

  • Put all 3 of the TV's in their respective windows in Level 188 in order to access the exit door. Then, jump in the pit in the room to get here.

Trivia

  • This Level only ever had one teaser, but it was for a Level -33.5 Area in the Dark Poolrooms.
  • The name for the Expanded Poolrooms was confirmed by Fancy on Discord. He also brought up the name Poolrooms Expanded.
  • Despite Fancy's 10th Devlog stating that this Level is called Level 37.2, searching for Level 37.2 leads to 0 results for the Sublevel. This is either an error on Fancy's end or the Level 37.2 page on Fandom got changed to what it is now and Fancy actually meant to say it was Level 37.1 or The Deep End. This is more accurate to the page he showed in the Devlog.
  • It was believed that the large mushroom rooms teased by Fancy meant Fancy would add Level -33.5, also known as flotadores which is floaters in Spanish from the Spanish Backrooms Wiki. Later, when Fancy released his 10th Devlog after Part 4 released this may or may not meant to have been a reference to it.
  • This Level does not have an Achievement for completing it or a Hub Entry.
  • There is a bug where the player can jump on the iron bar and land into the slides room, this way you can skip the Level.
