Appearance

Level 2 is a long, dark, grimy, thin maintenance tunnel that has pipes extending along the walls. The level stretches on for [how long specifically?]. Going left the first time leads to Level 3, and going left the second time leads to Level Fun.

Objective

Part 1: The Station

Once you first spawn in, you will spot a wall that points left. Going left leads to the Station, and going right leads to Fun. If you are entering this level for the first time, you will have to go left first. If you try to go right first, a Smiler will stop you from doing so and will be blocking the path until you complete the left path. After traveling for a bit, there will be a dark area with a Flashlight on a small shelf on the wall. Pick up this flashlight so you can see in the dark. Halfway down the hallway, two pipes will burst. Walking through the Steam will kill you in a similar way to the hydrolitis plague in level 37.2. Once you are almost in the light, a Smiler will start chasing you down, forcing you to run. When the Smiler enters areas with light, the lights will break, so if you hear lights breaking, it may already be too late. Eventually, you will find a turnoff for the entrance to the Station. Keep an eye out for it, because it is very easy to miss if you aren't paying attention. It will act as a safe zone from the Smiler, so once you enter the entrance, you no longer have to worry about the Smiler. If you pass the entrance, you will eventually find yourself in a dead end with nothing you can do.

Part 2: FUN =)

After escaping the boiler room in Level 5, you will find yourself at a different entrance to level 2 on the opposite side. The wall in front points left with the message "Fun!" Once you arrive at the darkness again, get the Flashlight and enter the darkness. The only thing that makes the right path different from the left one is that only one pipe will burst. The Smiler will chase you again once almost out of the darkness. Instead of finding an entrance to the station, you will find an entrance to Level Fun. There will also be flashing colorful lights, so you know this is the exit as well as an instrumental edition of You Day! playing. Touch a ladder and you will end up in Level Fun. Just like last time, if you pass the exit this time, you will once again find a dead end.

  • Find the correct door in the garage in Floor 4 in Level 1 to get here.

Update History

Release Added the level.
Part 2 Added the Level 3 exit and the ability to return to the level from Level 5 as well as the Flashlight on the floor.
Part 4 Shortened the level a bit and changed the music playing at the Level Fun entrance.
Version 1.0 Removed the fun arrow on the beginning wall and added the loading hallway to the beginning to add the ability to go back from both entrances. The entrance from level 5 is now to the right of the level 1 entrance on the opposite side, and is locked on the first visit. The wall there has only the fun arrow. Additionally, added a small shelf at the entrance to the dark area with the flashlight on it and the steam from the pipes in the dark area now kills you in a similar way to the hydrolitis plague. Finally, added a door which closes upon exit to Level 3, and the Level Fun exit now has multiple ladders.
Version 1.1 (2675) All the text in the level was removed.

Release Version

This level was one that was added on the game's Release. The level was nearly the same, except going left would just lead to a dead end with no Level 3 entrance. The wall text (and the wall itself) at the start was also gone because you had to go right. The Flashlight was automatic and would turn on the moment you entered the dark area, and the music in the Level Fun entrance was the old Row Row Row Your Boat instead of the You Day! Instrumental.

Trivia

  • This level is one of the few levels which has multiple main path entrances & exits, as well as the first level in the game to have multiple.
  • The ambience of this level is not made by Fancy or ins0mic but instead made by Baceero 2. You can find the original ambience that was used here.
  • If you enter Level 2 for the first time and go right, using cheats to get past the Smiler and reach the Level Fun entrance, prior to Version 1.0, it wasn't possible to enter Level Fun from the entrance, but after Version 1.0 it became possible.
  • Prior to Version 1.0, Gas was not deadly.
  • Fancy added Lighthead to the level to make it not as safe as walking forward but was soon replaced by the full body Smiler.
  • During the development of this level, prior to the addition of the entrance to Level Fun, the exit was a white light, likely just being a placeholder.
  • Level 2 in the original Backrooms lore goes by the name "Abandoned Utility Halls" on the Backrooms Wikidot and still has the name "Pipe Dreams" on the Backrooms Fandom Wiki.
