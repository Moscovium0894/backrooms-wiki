Appearance

The Manilla Room is a small room with grey walls, a grey carpet, some furniture, a Locker and a vent that leads to the rest of Level 0. The level also contains an entrance to Level 0.11.

Objective

Part 1: Figuring out the Locker Code

Once you first enter, you will notice that there are boarded up boards that lead to Level 0.11. You must get a Chainsaw to break down these boards, and there's a Locker that has a Chainsaw inside of it. In order to unlock it, you must go to the miniature Level 0.2 within the smaller area of Level 0, and you must use the Light Switch to see the shapes on the Locker. Every time you use the Light Switch, the shape will change, and the number that the shape is on is the order you put it into on the Locker.

Part 2: Entering Level 0.11

Once you have figured out the Locker code, go back to The Manila Room and put in the code, and the Locker will open. If you forget or need it for any other reason it's square, circle, arrow. On the other side is a small Level 0.11 room. After staying in this area for a few seconds, a cutscene will play of the player Noclipping through the ground. Then, you will find yourself in Level 0.11.

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Enter Level 0 from Level 8 to be in the smaller area, and within the area is The Manila Room.

Exits

  • Use the Chainsaw to cut down the boards that lead to Level 0.11 to get to Level 0.11.
  • If you have a Crowbar, you can use it to break open the Vent in the room, and on the other side is the rest of Level 0.

Trivia

  • In the game files and the patch notes of Hotfix #15, for some reason the level is spelled with 2 L's instead of 1 like on the Backrooms Wikidot and Fandom Wiki.
  • On the Fandom Wiki Page and the Wikidot Page, the descriptions of The Manilla Room are different compared to what is currently in the game.
