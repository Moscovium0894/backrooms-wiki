Appearance

Level 0.3 possesses similarities with Level 0, the only difference being that everything is Blue due to the Coldness and Ice of the Level. The Level has a Maze-like structure, and several Lockers are scattered throughout it. A Damaged Van can be found throughout the Level, which you must take a Picture of using a Camera.

Objective

Part 1: The Van

Once you get to the Layer from Level 55.1, you must have a Camera with you, and you must find a Damaged Van in the Level. Once you find it, take a Picture of it and the Photo on the Camera will change to the Bacteria.

Part 2: The Bacteria

Once you've taken a Photo of the Van, you must take a Photo of the Bacteria. A good way of doing this is by getting the Bacteria to spot you and hide in a Locker. When the Bacteria walks away, get out of the Locker and get a Photo of it. Once you have taken a Photo of the Bacteria, you can go to the Level 0.11 Layer to continue Level 55.1.
