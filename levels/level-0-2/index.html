Bone Thief Minimal

Bone Thieves are large entities with yellow skin and eyes inside of its mouth. They are weirdly shaped and are stationary entities that live inside dark holes in the wall. They kill any wanderers that come near their hole.

FIELD TIP — Flip the switch when powered on and run quickly to the exit.

Appearance

The M.E.G. Civilization

The M.E.G. Civilization resembles Level 0 but it's much smaller and less maze-like. Near the start, there's a map that suggests the red-carpet aesthetic that resembles Level 0.2 is being intentionally built and spread around by M.E.G.

Level 55.1 Layer

In Level 55.1, the first layer is a layer which resembles Level 0 but with white walls and a red carpet.

Objective

The M.E.G. Civilization

This is what you have to do in The M.E.G. Civilization.

Part 1: Turn on the Electricity Boxes

Once you first spawn in, you will be in a room with walls and carpet that resembles Level 0.2. Next to this room is a large area with a hole in the wall. This hole is where the Bone Thief lives. The exit to the level is on the other side of the Gate, however the Bone Thief will stop you from getting to it. To get to the Elevator, You must enter the rest of the level and look for Noclipping floors which are glitching areas in the ground. They are all marked with yellow tape and have Traffic Lights next to them. If the Traffic Light is green, that means there's no danger nearby the Noclipping floor, but if it's red, that means there's danger nearby and you should wait for it to turn green. Once you walk through a Noclipping floor, you will fall through and enter Level 0.1. Within the rest of the level, there are 5 doors, and they are all locked. In order to unlock them, you must go into Level 0.1 and open up the doors. If you've opened up a door, return to The M.E.G. Civilization and open whichever door is unlocked. On the other side is a small room, and each room looks different. They all contain Electricity Boxes which must be enabled to escape. On the Electricity Box is a moving arrow and a yellow area. You must stop the arrow in the yellow area to complete the puzzle. Turn on all 5 of the Electricity Boxes and return to the Bone Thief Area.

Part 2: Get to the Elevator

After turning on all 5 of the Electricity Boxes, the lever next to the Bone Thief area will be usable. Once pulled, a Gate will come down and it will keep the Bone Thief from getting out, however the gate will go back up once it reaches the bottom meaning you'll have to be quick to get to the Elevator. Once in the Elevator, it will close and go down. The Elevator leads to The Snackrooms.

Level 55.1

This is what you have to do in the Level 0.2 layer in Level 55.1.

Part 1: Who is Here?

Once you get to the layer from Level 55.1, you must have a Camera with you, and you must find a wall that says, "Who is Here?" in the level. Once you find it, take a picture of it and the photo on the Camera will change to a room that has a view of Level 0. If you are playing in Multiplayer, the "Who is Here?" photo will be a photo of another player.

Part 2: The Level 0 Entrance

After taking the first photo, you must take a photo of an area that has a view of Level 0. Find the area and take the photo. The photo will then change to a damaged van in Level 0.3. You can then go to the Level 0.3 Layer to continue Level 55.1.
