Threat assessment
Animation Dangerous

Animations resemble childlike wooden puppets built for kids in the 1930s with red glowing eyes. They spawn during the night to kill those who do not hide in the houses of Level 94 . They aren't the smartest entities, because while they know how to break into a house, they can't figure out hiding places.

FIELD TIP — Hide in houses as soon as you see the sun going down.

The Animated King Very Deadly

The Animated King is a plastic clown with green hair, red gloves, a purple and yellow clown outfit, and some green clown shoes. It is the Boss Entity of Level 94 .

FIELD TIP — Turn around all the time and look out for it.

Appearance

Level 94 is a vast expanse of dream-like green hills filled with different sorts of objects that a young kid would draw, including a water tower, houses as well as fences around them. The level also has a dreamcore vibe. The sky is mainly bright with the sun shining down on the level until nighttime. Trucks can be found on some of the streets that go throughout Level 94, and there are massive Sirens that play music that sounds as if it would come from an old 1900's cartoon.

Objective

Part 1: Get to the Castle

Once you first spawn in, there will be a sign pointing right. On this sign, there is a Note warning you about Animations. Traverse through Level 94 and hide in a house at night so you don't die to Animations and use Trucks to gain distance. Get in a Truck and it will start up, taking you for a little ride to get you closer and closer to the castle that you have to get to. The Trucks won't work forever though. In order to escape the neighborhood, you must reach the Animated Kingdom and climb the rope coming down from it to enter.

Part 2: The Animated King's Trial

Once you're in the castle, you will find yourself in a funhouse place with a locked door at the front of the room. In this funhouse, there are 4 Cubes that you can push around with shapes on them. One with a star, one with a circle, one with a square, and one with all of them. Push these Cubes into their place on the floor that has the same shape. If you get it correctly, the door will unlock. The door will close after entering the area. You will then enter a miniature amusement park with stuff like tables, and lampshades. In the center, there is a podium with the Animated King on it. Once looking away from it for a few seconds, he will disappear. Then the boss battle will begin. The lights will turn off and the Animated King will attempt to attack you when you're not looking at him, and when you do see him, he will disappear with some time until his next attack. There are also Liquid Pain dispensers, which every now and then will spawn Liquid Pain. Once another one has more Liquid Pain, the previous one's Liquid Pain will disappear. Survive for 100 seconds (1 minute and 40 seconds) and get on the Roller Coaster in order to escape and get to Level 6.

Level 94 - Motion

Day & Night

This level possesses a day/night cycle. Day lasts for 3 minutes and night lasts for 1 minute. But after the first night, day will last for 2 minutes. The day is perfectly safe while at night, hordes of Animations will come to kill anyone unfortunate enough to not find a house to hide inside of in time. The Animations will also break down doors of houses if they figure out you're in there, so hiding in one of the 2 Closets or under the Bed is the safest thing to do. When it becomes nighttime, the music from the Sirens will stop and Trucks will close their doors regardless if you are in them or not.

Drawers

In some of the beginning houses, you can find Drawers on the second floor that may contain Almond Water. They appear in less houses farther into the level.

Items

Items in this level are difficult to find, but you can still find some things. These items include:

  • Almond Water (found in Drawers and around Trucks)
  • Liquid Pain (found in the Animated King boss)
  • Flashlight (found in the Animated King boss on a Table)

Entities

This level only has 2 entities. These entities are:

  • Animations
  • The Animated King

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Once you have collected all the Tapes in The End, a door leading to a neighboring room will unlock. When you enter the room, there will be a ladder leaning against a wall, leading up to a Vent. Climb the Ladder and crawl through the Vent to get to this level.
  • After finishing the level, if you are in The Hub, you can enter this level through its door, and you will be in the very first house.

Exits

  • Survive 100 seconds (1 minute and 40 seconds) in the Animated King boss battle and get on the Roller Coaster to get to Level 6.

The Hub Door

To find this level's Hub Door, go inside the very first house in the level and go upstairs. Interact with the door up there and on the other side is the Door.

Trivia

  • This was the last level in the game in the game's Release.
    • After completing Level 9223372036854775807, a cutscene would play of a man waking up at a house and then going over to the window and see that outside is Level 94 (the early level 94). Then, the credits would roll. This Ending was brought back in a similar way with The Suburbs Ending.
  • This is currently the largest level in the game.
  • Level 94 has a total of 126 houses you can find.
  • There are locked doors in the second floors of every house, however only the one in the first house works and has a Hub Door behind it. All of the doors actually have a room behind them, but there's nothing in them.
  • There are two houses close to the Animated Kingdom in this level that are inside of each other.
  • You can beat this level right as it turns night for the first time if you get to the Animated Kingdom fast enough with really good movement.
  • Prior to Version 1.0, Level 9223372036854775807 was the entry point to this level.
  • In Fancy's 2nd Devlog, it was revealed that this level was going to be a secret level. This didn't end up happening, and it ended up becoming a level that requires you to complete to progress. Fancy could have also just called it a secret level because he was working on it secretly.
  • In Fancy's 3rd Devlog, he mentioned how he used a height map to create the landscape.
  • Before this level released, it looked very different and had different house designs. There were also no Roads, Trucks, Sirens, Water Tower, and Animated Kingdom.
  • In the pre-release version of Level 94, instead of Animations, countless amounts of Smilers would spawn, trapping you inside a house until the Sun came back up. This was changed due to people wanting Animations instead of Smilers.
  • The sound of the gate closing after entering the miniature amusement park is the same sound used for the gate opening sound in Level 3, but both levels have 2 separate audio files for both sounds.
  • Just like Level 6 and Level 0.11, pictures of this level were used on Level 55's page on the Backrooms Fandom wiki, although the pictures originate from an unofficial Escape the Backrooms Wiki and as a result are credited to it.
    • Something interesting about pictures of Level 94 being used for a page of Level 55 is that a level called Level 55.1 exists in the game but bears zero resemblance to Level 94.
