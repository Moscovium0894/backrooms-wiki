Appearance

Level 0.11 looks just like Level 0 but it's extremely rundown and abandoned. The level also has rooms that contain furniture such as chairs and tables. There's also a smaller version of this level in the final layer in Level 55.1.

Objective

Part 1: Escape the Maze

Once you first spawn in, you will find yourself in a maze with no way to go back to Level 0. Look around for Spray-Painted arrows on the walls and follow them to escape the maze.

Part 2: Unlock the Gate

After escaping the maze, you will find yourself in a much smaller area that resembles a bunch of hallways with pillars and doors. These doors will appear to have boards on them, so use a Chainsaw to destroy the boards and enter the room. The objective at this point is to find 4 Levers in these rooms and flip them all, but they will slowly unwind which means if you take too long, you will have to flip a Lever again. Keep an eye out for the Bacteria as well, because it is roaming around this level. If it spots you, you have to find a Vent and hide in it so it leaves you alone. Flip all 4 Levers and go to the gate beyond the main area. If all of the Levers are flipped, the gate should be fully open. On the other side, there is a door that leads to the Level 188 Courtyard, and a Lever that is used to open the gate again which is useless in singleplayer but can come in handy when playing with multiple players multiplayer.

Part 3: The Courtyard

Once you enter the Level 188 courtyard, proceed forward and enter the hotel area. This small hotel area has an Elevator that will let you leave the level, but it has a Color-Coded Lock. Look at the windows and the layout of the open windows is the code. The code is always the same and it is 415314. Type this into the keypad, and the Elevator should open. Enter the Elevator and press the buttons in it, and you will be back at Level 8.

Trivia

  • This level has gone through many changes. Originally, this level was actually the rest of the Level 188 courtyard, and Fancy thought it looked too generic. Then, he added Level 🗿 but just ended up making Level 0.11 instead because of how similar they look. However, the Moai Statues were still included throughout the Level in open areas. After a while of the Statues being in the level, he thought they looked absolutely ridiculous, and then he just deleted them all. The statues still somehow made a small cameo in the Level 0.11 layer in Level 55.1, and they are one of the things you must take a picture of in the Sub-Level.
  • In Multiplayer, the host is always the one that will spawn at the player spawn in Singleplayer.
  • The reason why you don't get an Achievement for completing this Sub-Level is because of the original idea that this level was originally going to be the rest of the Level 188 courtyard.
  • When Part 3 came out, the Level 188 courtyard was originally Level 188, but in Part 4, a completed Level 188 was added, making the Level 188 courtyard a part of Level 0.11.
  • The fireplace in the hotel area in the Level 188 courtyard doesn't have sound, but fans on Level 1 do have sound when getting close to them.
  • The ambience for this level is the same as Level 0's Ambience, but with more unnerving sounds included.
  • The Level 188 courtyard is connected to the level because it does not have a loading screen.
  • Just like Level 94 and Level 6, the main render of this level and a picture from this wiki page were used on a rewrite of Level 0.11's page on the Backrooms Fandom wiki.
