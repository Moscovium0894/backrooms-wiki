Appearance

The Castle looks like a large Castle with a yellowish white tone that sits on a Cloud. The Castle has 2 rooms on the inside, which include the Funhouse, and the Miniature Amusement Park.

Levels

The Castle can be found in:

  • Level 94

Inside

The Funhouse

The first room in the Castle is the Funhouse. The Funhouse appears to be a room with Pillars and circus themed objects. The Funhouse has many decorations like a cannon, boxes, paintings, bowling pins and much more. There is a large door in this room that looks like a Clown that will block the way to the Miniature Amusement Park. To open the Door, you must solve a Puzzle involving putting Cubes on their corresponding Shape. If you do it right, the Door will open, and it leads to the Miniature Amusement Park.

The Miniature Amusement Park

The Miniature Amusement Park is a massive room with many objects like streetlights, Liquid Pain Dispensers, a Roller Coaster, and The Animated King. If you complete The Animated King's Trial (which requires you to survive for 2 minutes or 100 seconds) you can get on the Roller Coaster and get to Level 6.
