Threat assessment
Female Deathmoth Dangerous

Female Deathmoths are black, white, and gray Moths that are massive in size and have a mark on their back that vaguely resembles a skull.

FIELD TIP — Crouch if you're in a room with a Female Deathmoth inside.

Skin-Stealer Deadly

Skin-Stealers are humanoids with a mouth that goes down to their body. Its skin is gray, and it has 2 glowing white eyes. There are also various blood spots on it mainly around its mouth. Skin-Stealers possess the unique ability to steal a player's skin after killing them and disguise itself as a wanderer.

FIELD TIP — Run away from any Players that appear to be chasing you.

Appearance

Level 8 is a massive underground cavern made of tunnels that twist and turn like most caves. Most of the level is completely shrouded in darkness, but you can still see without a Flashlight in these areas.

Objective

Part 1: Sneak past the Deathmoths

Once you first spawn in, you will find yourself at the entrance. There is a table with some Items as well as a new Item: Walkie Talkies. Make sure to get a Flashlight to navigate the place better. Enter the transporter and turn the valve to turn it on. Bug Spray will come out, and then the door will open, sending you into the cave. In the first two areas, you must sneak past Female Deathmoths that are around the place. Look around until you find the next transporter. All the transporters look the same, so make sure you're in the right one.

Part 2: The Bridge

The second area looks the same, but you must find a bridge. The bridge is not in good condition due to its believed age, so running on it will result in it to break and kill you. Either walk or crouch on it so it doesn't break. Jumping on it can also result in it to break. On the other side, there is a table with some Items on it as well as the next transporter. Enter the transporter to get sent to the third area. If you do not want to take the bridge to get across, there is another path you can take. To the right of the bridge start and up the rocks is a tunnel which will take you to the other side and past the transporter. However, it is not a good idea to take this path if as it will prevent you from getting the Items on the table on the other side of the bridge and you won't be able to hide from Skin-Stealers in the transporter.

Part 3: Survive the Skin-Stealers

In the third area, you have to survive two Skin-Stealers that have Flashlights. This area is the same as before, just find the transporter to arrive at the final area.

Part 4: Get to the Other Side

In the final area, you'll find a ditch that will stop your journey. You must climb a ladder that is in this final area to get back to Level 0. In Level 0, you will be in the smaller area of the Level, and you must complete Level 0.11 and take the Elevator in the Level 188 Courtyard. Once the Elevator ride is done you will be on the other side of the ditch (the ditch is blocked off by invisible walls). Take the available path and go up the pathway that it leads to, and you'll arrive at Level 9.

Closing Paths

While traversing Level 8, be cautious of the pathways that can collapse with rocks. This can happen once in the third area. Once you walk under the unsecure area the cave ceiling will collapse, and you or the rest of your crew if you're playing in Multiplayer must find another way around.

Items

Items in this level can be found on two tables that are in the level, and in transporters. These items Include:

  • Almond Water
  • Juice
  • Energy Bar
  • Flashlight
  • Walkie Talkie (first appearance)

Entities

Entities in this level are pretty dangerous and numerous. These entities include:

  • Female Deathmoth (8)
  • Skin-Stealer (2)

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Find the cave entrance in the underwater of Level 7 to get here.
  • Use the elevator in the Level 188 Courtyard in Level 0.11 to get here.

Exits

  • Use the ladder on the other side of the pit in the final area to return to Level 0.
  • If you're on the other side of the pit, you can follow the path to get to Level 9.

Trivia

  • This level was originally shown to have not just Skin-Stealers and Deathmoths but also include Hounds and Wretches.
  • The entities in this level would originally spawn in different locations so you wouldn't be able to memorize the way to go. When the level came out in Part 3, the locations of entities are not different, but specific.
  • This level was one that wasn't teased on Fancy's Discord Server, but it was teased on his YouTube Channel.
  • There was a bug where you could fly over the ditch in the final area and get to the Level 9 exit even if you're not on the side with the Elevator.
