Appearance

Level 3 is a large and abandoned electrical station with red brick walls and a dusty stone floor. The level has three different zones which are all locked off by gates. The farther you go into the level, the more dangerous it gets. This level is the main habitat of the Hounds.

Objective

Part 1: East Substation

Once you first spawn in, you will find yourself in a very long hallway, and at the end are locked bars. These bars lead to Level 4, so you will need to unlock them in order to escape. There is a box next to the locked bars containing a list of Fuse Boxes that must be turned on. The entrance door to the rest of the level will be locked at first but will open when you get near the box. You will then find yourself in the first zone, the east substation. In this zone, you must go around and look for Fuse Boxes. Enable all 3 of the Fuse Boxes in this zone, and more locked bars at the entrance of zone 1 will open allowing you to move to the second zone.

The Normal Fuse Box

All you have to do is flip all of the switches, and it will be enabled.

Part 2: West Substation

The closed bars at the start of zone 1 lead to this big room with red and white bricks, and 2 more closed-off bars, however the one at the start will be open. These bars lead to zone 2, the west substation. zone 2 is much darker and dangerous than the first zone, and once again you must find 3 Fuse Boxes and turn them on. After travelling for a bit, you will come across a hallway. But before you get the chance to open the door in this hall, a Hound will come up and start chasing you, so make sure you have a Flashlight out to blind it. There are only 2 Hounds in this zone, so it's not that hard.

Part 3: Generator

Zone 3 known as Generator is the most dangerous zone of them all. After turning on all 3 of the Fuse Boxes in zone 2, the other bars in the big room will be open. The third zone is very dark, so make sure you have a Flashlight out to see. The first room in zone 3 will already have a Hound. Hounds aren't the only danger in this zone, because a new hazard is introduced which are simply puddles with electricity in them. All you have to do to avoid them is to just not step in them. Simple as that. What makes the zone even harder is that the Fuse Boxes are now puzzles. There are only 2 kinds of puzzles though, which include:

Triway

This puzzle involves a bunch of electricity connectors which have 3 fuses on each one and all of the connectors form a hexagon shape. Every one of the fuse colors are different, but there's only 3 colors they can be which include green, blue, and red. You can turn the connectors and if 2 of the same fuse colors are aiming at each other, electricity will form. The goal is to get them all to match up.

Colored Wires

This puzzle is much simpler and easier than Triway. On the left side, there will be a bunch of wires, and they are each labelled with different colors. The colors include green, blue, red, and yellow. On the right side lies what the wires must be connected to. Connect the correct colors with each one and that's it.

Part 4: All of the Fuse Boxes Finally on

After turning on all of the Fuse Boxes in zone 3, the bars from the start of the level will be open, and you will be able to get to Level 4.

Items

Items in this level are hidden on shelves and are difficult to spot, and they can also be found on tables. These items Include:

  • Almond Water
  • Energy Bar (first appearance)
  • Flashlight

Entities

Only one type of entity lives in this level, and they are everywhere. This entity is:

  • Hound (9) (first appearance)

Objects

Level 3 has two objects. These objects include:

  • Fuse Box
  • Door

Hazards

One hazard can be found in the level in zone 3. This hazard is:

  • Electric Puddle

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Go left in Level 2 until you find the entrance to this level.

Exits

  • Turn on all 9 Fuse Boxes and go through the door that lies beyond the bars at the start of the level to get to Level 4.

Trivia

  • The further you go into Level 3, the darker and more dangerous the level gets.
  • 3 Hounds in this level will despawn after shining a Flashlight on them, however the second one you'll come across in zone 3 doesn't despawn if you're playing on Hard or Nightmare in Multiplayer.
  • It took Fancy a really long time to decorate the various rooms in the level.
  • In the original Backrooms lore, Level 3 has much more entities than just Hounds.
