Trivia

  • Originally, this Level was actually the rest of the Level 188 Courtyard, and Fancy thought it looked too generic. Then, he added Level 🗿 but just ended up making Level 0.11 instead because of how similar they look. However, the Moai Statues were still included throughout the Level in open areas. After awhile of the Statues being in the Level, he thought they looked absolutely ridiculous, and then he just deleted them all, resulting in the Level to become Level 0.11.
  • This is currently the only Level to have been Scrapped.
  • In the Level 0.11 layer of Level 55.1, there are Moai Statues present, which is likely a reference to this Level.
