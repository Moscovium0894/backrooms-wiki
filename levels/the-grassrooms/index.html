Appearance

The Grassrooms is a large indoor concrete complex with the grounds made of grass and flowers. The Level is filled with windows that let you see the outside.

Objective

Part 1: The First Rooms

Once you first spawn in, you're going to want to explore around until you find a large room with a nearly entirely grassy ground and 2 levers. Pull these levers once to move the bridge pieces across to the other side. Upon getting on one, it will take you to the other side. On the other side of these bridge pieces is a concrete block with Ropes and a wooden plank being held up by a rope. Pull a lever that's on this concrete block, and it will unlock a metal door from below. Enter this door and inside are some Knives. Pick up one and return to the concrete block. Then, use the Knife to cut the rope on the plank and walk on the plank to the next room. In the next room, go up the staircase, and at the top is another rope you can cut. Cut it and a wood piece drops on glass below, opening the next area.

Part 2: M.E.G. Buttons

In the next area, there is a metal door locked off by 4 red lights. To make the lights green, go through the maze that's right next to the door and press all 4 of the M.E.G. operated buttons. Once all 4 are pressed, the metal door will open. On the other side is a large room with platforming structures. On here, you must find another rope holding up a plank and cut it with a Knife. This will open up the path to the next and final room. In this last room, you must press 4 M.E.G. operated buttons to unlock a metal door once again, but instead of finding the buttons in the maze, you must find a staircase that lets you walk on top of a bit of the maze allowing you to get to a more isolated staircase that leads up to an upper area containing the 4 buttons. Press them all and return to the metal door. It will be opened, and on the other side is the exit Elevator, however upon entering, you'll notice it has a very large number of buttons. As soon as you enter the Elevator, you will get the Empty City Ending.

Endings

Empty City

Upon getting in the Elevator, a cutscene plays where it starts travelling down through a lot floors and arrives at the 11th floor, which appears as a city. Strangely enough, there's no one there, and the player calls out for people just for there to be nothing. You will then wake up in the Bunker.

Items

Three items can be found in this level, two being returning items and the other being new. These items include:

  • Walkie Talkie
  • Rope
  • Knife (first appearance)

Entities

There are no Entities in this Level.

Objects

Only one object can be seen in this Level, which is taken from the previous level, Overgrowth to get here. This object is:

  • Slides

Hazards

One main hazard can be found in this Level. This Hazard is:

  • Vines

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Disarm all 4 Death Slides in Overgrowth to open the right slide that leads to this level.

Exits

  • Ride the Elevator at the end of the level to get the Empty City Ending.
