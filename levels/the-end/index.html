Appearance

The End is a trap level made to trick unsuspecting wanderers into thinking they have escaped the backrooms. The Level is presumably a "web" created by its only Entity. The level resembles an abandoned library with no books on its shelves. There is also a Computer in the center desk in the level that turns on whenever you spawn into the level.

Objective

Once you first spawn in, you will find yourself in a box-like library room, with the only possible exit being a locked door. To unlock it, go around the level and collect Tapes you will find on bookshelves, avoiding the Scratcher that aimlessly roams the room. The Computer turns on once you spawn into the level and it has a number on it of how many Tapes need to be collected. The amount of Tapes needed depends on how many people you're playing with. If it's one person, there is 24 Tapes to be collected, if it's 2 people, there is 32 Tapes to be collected, and if it's 3 or 4 people, there is 48 Tapes to be collected. Once all of the Tapes are collected, go up to the Computer on the center desk and turn it back on. This will unlock the door, which leads to a much more damaged room with a Ladder. Enter the room and climb up the Ladder to reach a Vent that leads to Level 94.

Lights Out Event

After being in the level for 90 seconds, the lights will turn off, forcing the player to use a Flashlight to collect the rest of the Tapes.

Surviving

There are two Tables in this level and two Fire Alarms you can use to stay alive in this level.

Tables

If you get caught by the Scratcher, you can make a run for a Table and hide underneath it. After waiting for a few seconds, the Scratcher will give up and leave you alone.

Fire Alarms

If you want to distract the Scratcher so you can collect some Tapes, you can use one of the two Fire Alarms in the level. The Fire Alarms are located on the complete opposite side of each other on the left and right side of the room. Use a Fire Alarm, and the Scratcher will go towards it, giving you time to collect the Tapes. Keep in mind that you can only use them so often and after using one, it takes 3 minutes to use them again. If you got caught by the Scratcher and want to use a Fire Alarm, the Fire Alarms won't do you any good.

Release Version

This level was one that was added on the game's Release. The level was nearly the same, except the lights didn't go off, the Scratcher had its original model, you couldn't hide underneath Tables, there were no Fire Alarms, and there were no items, Note, or disk player. The most notable difference was that once you turned on the Computer, a video would play that lasts about 2 minutes. It talks about visual form agnosia and how The World Insecure Unconscious Neuroscience was looking for participants to help them understand more about visual form agnosia. The Orb also spawned back then instead of the second room which took you to Level 9223372036854775807 after the video was played, or after it was finished.

Trivia

  • According to Fancy's Devlogs, this level is based off of the Fandom wiki Version and not the Wikidot Version.
  • This level in the game's Release was actually extremely hard due to the lack of Fire Alarms and being unable to hide under Tables meaning that if the Scratcher caught you, it was game over. The lights also didn't go out either.
  • Originally, Fancy thought this was the last level of the Backrooms, but then everyone told him about Level 9223372036854775807. After making that level, everybody then said that the actual final level is Level 3999.
  • This was initially supposed to be the first level in one of the Ending paths in Version 1.0, but for some reason this was changed to The Hub.
