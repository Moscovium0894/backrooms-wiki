Trivia

  • This is currently the smallest level in the game.
  • Considering the name, it seems like the intended way of getting to this level is by cheating, but it's actually possible to get to it by not cheating. What you have to do is in the exit room of Level 188, hide behind the glass panels and wait for the Aranea Membri to get to the other side of the room. If it's far away enough, make a run for the door. Be sure you're as close to the door as possible when you head to it to avoid the Aranea Membri getting to you.
  • This is the only level in the game without anywhere to progress to.
  • This level is entirely hidden from everywhere in the game and is not mentioned anywhere as the only way to get to it is by getting to the door the Aranea Membri blocks in the Level 188 room that leads to Level 37.2.
  • The first and only teaser of this level appears slightly different to how the completed level looks.
  • Just like the Partypoopers, This level was blacklisted from being suggested on Fancy's Discord server.
    • This was due to the fact that people wanted it to be an anti-cheat level but Fancy didn't think it made sense to have an entire level if a player decides to cheat. The workaround to add it was to have it as a normal level you can find instead of one you get to by cheating, which makes the name feel a little pointless.
  • Fancy almost talked about this level in his 12th Devlog but was interrupted by Maic.
  • It was revealed in Fancy's 13th Devlog that this level will have a proper objective in which players have to go through keys to find the right one to the door and if they take too long, they will die to whatever is behind the door. Although, in the Devlog, it was said that the level isn't in the game at all despite the fact that it is but incomplete.
