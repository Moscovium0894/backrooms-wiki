Appearance

Level 4 is an abandoned office building with little trace of people. Sanity doesn't drop in this level, making it a good place to stay at. There are multiple rooms, with standard office cubicles. Most rooms are locked, though. There's also lightning that will strike outside frequently in the level.

Objective

Part 1: The Vending Machines

Once you first spawn in, look around for a room with 4 different vending machines. The hallway leading to it has 2 Window entities on the ends, so don't go up to them. There will be a door in this room that will let you proceed on, but it's locked. In order to unlock it, you have to use the vending machines in the room. Every vending machine is associated with an object. The objects Include chairs, tables, water coolers, and stacks of books. Count how many of that object is in the room, and on the vending machines, instead of buttons for a certain object, there are buttons that display numbers. Select the number of objects on each one and if all the values are right, the door should open.

Part 2: Sneak past the Cameras

After the vending machine puzzle, you will find yourself in a room full of cubicles. Here, you must find the room (which is located at the very end of the room) that leads to this hallway with a wall that has a button on it. The button says "Reset Cameras". Press this Button, and then you must sneak past 4 different Cameras scattered throughout a bunch of rooms. Sneaking past the Cameras is impossible without being spotted, so you must take shortcuts in order to get past them. If you get spotted by one, the Camera with flash red and an alarm will start playing. You'll eventually arrive at this brick hallway with a security office connected to it that has Crowbars on the desk and monitors that monitor the cameras. These monitors will say "SPOTTED!" If you were noticed by a Camera. If one of them says "SPOTTED!", the door that will take you to Level 5 will be locked, and you will have to take a Crowbar and break open the Vent in the hallway to get back to the beginning to try again. Don't get seen by any Cameras, and the door should open. It will take you to Level 5.

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Go through the door beyond the bars at the start of Level 3 to get here.

Exits

  • Don't get caught by a Camera in the final area of the level to get access to the door in the hallway at the end. This will send you to Level 5.
  • Find The Hub Door in the pillar room to get to The Hub.

The Hub Door

To find The Hub Door on this level, at the start of the level, instead of going right, go forward, and you should arrive at this room with pillars. Then, open the second door on the left side of the room and The Hub will be on the other side.

Trivia

  • The Hub Entry for this level mentions Window entities with the text "Any windows that have not been blacked out are considered traps and should be avoided at all costs", which before Version 1.0, there were no Window entities but now there are. The way The Hub Entry describes the Window entities isn't that accurate to how they are in-game however because no windows in the level have light.
  • In each of the main sets of windows in this area there is an image of other distinct levels that are too blurry to properly make out due to the rain and lightning. These prospective “levels” appear to range from an art gallery, a subway station, a mall, or an airport of some sort. The still image of each of the levels is a texture which repeats throughout all of the windows, and moves with the player’s vision of it.
  • In Fancy's 4th Devlog, he mentioned this level having different seasons but said that it rains 99% of the time, however in-game there's only rain.
