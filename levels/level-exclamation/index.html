Smiler Moderate

Smilers are floating smiling faces with sharp teeth with dark shadowy fog surrounding their bodies. Smilers are mysterious and shadow-like entities that are typically seen in pitch black places where only their smiling faces can be seen.

FIELD TIP — Try to stay in the light.

Partygoer Dangerous

Partygoers are yellow humanoid creatures with blood being easily noticeable on the mouth, hands, and legs. They hold Balloons and they stay in the same position, but if they spot you they will start chasing you. In Level Fun+ , they walk around the place and don't just stand in one place.

FIELD TIP — Crouch when trying to get around one.

Appearance

Level ! is a long hospital hallway with red lights and the loud sound of hospital sirens in all directions. The hallway is fairly short, with various objects such as hospital equipment and beds scattered that block your way.

Objective

Once you first spawn in, you must enter the hallway, and you will get chased by a Smiler and a group of 6 Partygoers. You will then have to run down the hallway and as you go through the Level, doors will open up and act as obstacles. Eventually, the path will be blocked off and you will have to take an open path on the left to proceed on. In this hallway, one of the Partygoers will enter it and chase you. Shortly after leaving this hallway you should arrive at the exit door. Push it and enter it and then you will fall down a hole and get to The End.

Trivia

  • This level was originally supposed to be a very skinny hallway. People disliked it, which resulted in it becoming a wider hospital hallway. The original version was reused for Level !~!.
  • The prototype obstacles for this level were cubes that would fall from the ceiling.
  • This level was the first one to be teased by Fancy in his Discord server.
  • The hallway in this level is very short despite being 10 km long in the original lore.
  • If you use cheats to avoid the entities, the level will restart when they reach the end.
  • If you use freecam, you can see hospital rooms behind some inaccessible doors.
