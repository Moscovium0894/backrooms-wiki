Appearance

Overgrowth looks just like Level 0, however it's very damaged and abandoned. The level has things like beige wallpaper and moist carpet just like Level 0, graffiti all over the walls, missing ceiling tiles, damaged ceiling tiles, bacteria deposits over the walls and ground, and air with bacteria making it hard to breathe in, which has caused previous Wanderers to pass out. This level is rumored to be where the Bacteria entity first evolved.

Objective

Part 1: Getting the gates open

Once you first spawn in, you'll be in the small built in the level. There are 3 locked gates and they lead to the rest of the level. To unlock it, head to the Death Slides and enter the room on the right of them. In it, there is a glass view of a Bone Thief hole and it can be reached through the Death Slides. To unlock the gates, pull the lever in this room and the gates will open.

Part 2: Death Slide Disarming

In order to escape the level, you must disarm the Death Slides from danger, and in order to do that you have to roam around the level and pick up passed out Wanderers and push them down the Slides. Be careful finding the Wanderers, as a large amount of Bacterias roam around the level. If one spots you, just hide in a hole in the wall, and it will leave. If a Slide isn't disarmed, riding it will lead you to a deadly fan. If a Slide is disarmed, the fan will be out of the way, but you'll end up in front of the Bone Thief hole and die. Find all 4 of the passed out Wanderers and use them to disarm all 4 Death Slides. Once this is done, ride down anyone, and they will instead send you to The Grassrooms.

Trivia

  • This is the only secret level in the game that is a sublevel, has returning entities, drains Sanity, and it's the only level with multiple Bacterias.
  • The graffiti seen throughout the walls of the level that have words include:

    • A robot
    • A smiley face
    • A skull
  • This is one of the only sublevels to not have a level number. The other sublevels without one includes Level !~! and Level Fun+.
    • Due to this, The Hub Mode description of the level mentions this being an unknown sublevel of Level 0.
  • In the announcement post for Version 1.0's release date, this level was described with the summary "discover more on the origins of the Bacteria in Overgrowth".
  • In Fancy's 12th Devlog and the level Achievement, this level is called The Death Slides. It is unknown why it's called Overgrowth in-game. It might be because the Slides aren't relevant enough for them to be the title to be fitting.
  • Overgrowth is not an actual Backrooms level. The level seems to have been made to have a level for the Death Slides instead of adding them to Level 0 like seen on the Wikidot page in Fancy's 12th Devlog.
    • This would also explain why this level is rumored to be the origin of the Bacteria entity, as the Bacteria is a Backrooms entity made for Kane Pixel's Backrooms Found Footage series on YouTube and not an entity on the Backrooms Fandom Wiki or the Wikidot.
