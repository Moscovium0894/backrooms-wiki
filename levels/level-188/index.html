Threat assessment
Aranea Membri Deadly

The Aranea Membri is a massive, spider-like creature with a skull of an unknown animal for a head. It has 4 large legs that gives it a high level of speed.

FIELD TIP — Run as fast as you can away from it into a hotel room if seen.

Windows Minimal

Window entities are grey humanoid entities with bright red eyes and long claws. They seem to be very bony and skinny with reptile-like skin. This entity stays behind a window and will attempt to lure the player closer to them.

FIELD TIP — Stay away from the window until you know that there isn't a window entity there.

Appearance

Level 188 is a hotel with a small courtyard, grey/black carpets, grey walls, and a grey ceiling with small white lights. The Level has a lobby with a desk and some Items scattered around, and a very small courtyard with a hole can be seen outside 3 windows.

Objective

Part 1: Collect the TV's

Once you first spawn in, you will be in the lobby. Go forward until you find an open Elevator. Get in the Elevator and use the buttons to go up and down a floor. The first floor is The Lobby, and the second is The Hotel. Go to The Hotel and look through the hotel rooms to find TV's. Each TV is labelled with 3 different letters: A, B, and C. The TV's can be found in bathrooms, in the open areas of The Hotel, and in hotel rooms. The Aranea Membri is roaming around the halls though, so if it spots you, make sure to hide in a hotel room. The Window Entity can also be seen in the hotel rooms. The TV's can be moved around very slowly, and once one is obtained, you must move it to the start and into an elevator next to the entry one with Electric Puddles in them. The TV will enter the Elevator and the Elevator will go down to The Lobby. Once going down to The Lobby, the TV will be out of the Elevator. You must then move it over to where the desk is, and below the 3 windows that show the very small courtyard are indicators for where to put each TV. Put all 3 down and the metal door that leads to the very small courtyard will open.

Part 2: The Very Small Courtyard

The very small courtyard features a hole and if you decide to go forward beyond the hole, a small area can be found with a door, which leads to Level You Cheated. If you try to go here, the Aranea Membri will come down from a Vent and start chasing you, so you will have to jump down the hole which leads to Level 37.2. In order to access the door, you can hide behind railings after activating the Aranea Membri and enter the level.

Items

Items in this Level are just the typical survival stuff. These Items include:

  • Almond Water
  • Juice
  • Energy Bar
  • Flashlight
  • Walkie-Talkie

Entities

Only 2 Entities live in this Level, and they both live in the Hotel Floor. These Entities are:

  • Aranea Membri (first appearance)
  • Windows

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Enter the final blue hallway in Level !~! at the top of the staircase to find a door that leads here to get here.
  • Use the corridor in Level You Cheated to return to Level 188.

Exits

  • Unlock the metal door by placing all 3 of the TV's and jump down the hole in the very small courtyard to get to Level 37.2.
  • Get past the Aranea Membri which guards the wooden doors to get to Level You Cheated.

The Hub Door

Finding The Hub Door in this Level is easy. Once you enter the Level, you will find a room with doors that have labels of different Levels. The only one you can access is a Door labelled with the letter "H". This Door leads to a small hallway with the door being at the end.

Trivia

  • The Courtyard in this Level is designed after a real place, the Holiday Inn at Terminal 4 of Heathrow Airport in London England.
  • A smaller version of this Level can be found in Level 0.11 at the end of the Level.
  • The Courtyard has 114 windows, which is not enough to fill out all the Levels in the game with them since Fancy mentioned in his 7th Devlog that every window would eventually be another Level for him to add.
    • The game currently only has 33 major Levels, which means if you started filling out the windows with the Levels on the left or right side, you wouldn't even be able to complete either of the sides since both of them have 42 windows each. The front side only has 30 windows however, which is more than how many Levels there are in the game.
  • You won't get the Escape Level 188 Achievement for completing Level 188 because it is given for escaping Level 0.11 since Level 188 was once the Level 188 Courtyard in Level 0.11.
  • Originally, Level 0.11 was entirely going to look like Level 188 instead of just having the Courtyard at the end, but later on Fancy thought it looked way too boring and changed it to look like Level 0.11 but never changed the last area featuring the Courtyard likely to keep them as 2 separate Levels. In Part 4, he added what is now entirely Level 188.
  • Sometimes the Elevator will take you to floor 3 of The Hotel. This can happen randomly. floor 3 is identical to floor 2, and you will be brought up to the higher balcony when you get there.
  • If you are holding an Item in front of a bathroom mirror, you will only see the player's hands.
