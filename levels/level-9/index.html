Appearance

Level 9 is a suburban neighborhood at night with houses containing Items. The streets are the most dangerous part of the level, and there are Wretches that will guard 6 houses.

Objective

Part 1: Turn on the Terminals

Once you first spawn in, explore the neighborhood until you find the big house at the top of the level. This house will be guarded off with a fence, as it stores The Abandoned Outpost inside. There will be a Terminal including a mini map of the level, with 3 different houses highlighted red (the houses will be different every time). Go to these houses and find the Computers inside of them. Turn them on, and make sure to be careful, because there might be a Wretch in a house. Once they're all on, go back to the big house and the gate will be unlocked. In the house, find the Spray-Painted arrow pointing at a door. Enter this door, and you will arrive at The Abandoned Outpost.

Part 2: Get the Administrator Card

In The Abandoned Outpost, you must find 4 bottles of Almond Concentrate and use them to return the Wretch who is roaming around the place back to normal. With this, you can get the administrator card it has and leave the Outpost. With the card, go to the back of the level. You should find a gate with a Administrator Card Terminal next to it. Use the Administrator Card on the Terminal to unlock the gate and get to Level 10.

Bases & Outposts

Level 9 only has one outpost that exists within the level. This outpost is:

  • The Abandoned Outpost

Male Deathmoths

Sometimes, you may spot some Male Deathmoths underneath a streetlight, but you might not be able to reach them. Male Deathmoths can also be spotted in a house. If you have Bug Spray, you can use it to get Moth Jelly.

Items

This Level has a ton of items you can find. These items include:

  • Almond Water
  • Juice
  • Energy Bar
  • Liquid Pain
  • Flashlight
  • Walkie Talkie
  • Bug Spray
  • Moth Jelly (can be obtained by using Bug Spray on Male Deathmoths)

Entities

There are only three entities in this level. These entities are:

Used Entities

  • The Neighborhood Watch (1) (first appearance)
  • Wretch (6)
  • Male Deathmoth (6)

Scrapped Entities

  • Jerry (first appearance)

The Hub Door

To find The Hub Door in this level, you must find a certain house at the start of the level. This house has a door that leads to a concrete staircase, and at the bottom is the Door. Keep in mind that the house does have a Wretch guarding upstairs.

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Unlock the Elevator in the Level 188 courtyard in Level 0.11 and use it to get back to Level 8. Then, go up the path to get here.

Exits

  • Use the Administrator Card at the back gate to get to Level 10.
  • Find the basement in the center house to get to The Abandoned Outpost.
  • Find the house with the basement to find a Door to The Hub.

Trivia

  • This level took Fancy multiple months to make, which explains why it's so long.
  • People really seemed to like this level when it came out due to its length and quality.
  • Even though this level is pretty small in size, it still is one of the longest to complete.
  • This level was one of the most teased by Fancy.
    • There was a teaser of the level, but it was blurred. The location of this teaser is unknown.
    • There was a video showing The Neighborhood Watch.
    • And there was a teaser of one of the 20+ houses you can explore in the level.
  • The Note you can find in this level can be found twice.
  • Level 9 has the highest Sanity risk in the entire game.
  • This level has the most Items that can be found in the entire game, beating Level 5 by one Item (unless you count each color of Glowstick in Level Fun+ as a different Item).
  • Upstairs, in the center house, you can find a bedroom with a red light. This might be a reference to Level 974, since it's a pink house and has you start in a bedroom, but it could also possibly be a reference to Jerry, an entity that was going to be in the level but got removed.
  • The Moon in this level is actually fully 3D.
  • There are 25 houses in this level (including the big house in the center).
  • There are three explorable houses that cannot have a Computer. Two are safe, and the third has a Wretch. (One of the safe ones is the big house)
  • There is a strategy where you lead a Wretch to the door and close it. This will push it outside their barrier. It can't go back in.
