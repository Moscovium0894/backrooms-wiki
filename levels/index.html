Backrooms Field Manual

Levels

Main route

Level 0 Level 0 is a non-linear space, resembling the back rooms of a retail outlet. Similar to its previous form, all rooms in level 0 appear uniform and share superficial features such as yellowed wallpaper, damp carpets, and inconsistently places fluorescent lighting. However, no two rooms within the Level are identical. Class 1 2 ENT Level 1 — The Habitable Zone Level 1 is a large, sprawling warehouse that features concrete floors and walls, exposed rebar, and a low-hanging fog with no discernable source. Class 2 6 ENT The Hub The Hub is a large underground tunnel that has Doors to most of the Levels in the game. There are 10 of these Doors along with an abandoned M.E.G. Base . Habitable 1 ENT Level 2 — Pipe Dreams Level 2 consists mainly of dark, grey, concrete maintenance tunnels, stretching on for miles with walls lined with pipes. Class 2 2 ENT Level 3 — Electrical Station The walls are comprised of dusty, brown bricks, usually covered in segments of copper pipes and mechanical components. The floors are made of an even dustier grey-tiled floor, whilst the ceiling is flaking paint in most areas. Class 4 1 ENT Level 4 — Abandoned Office Level 4 resembles an empty office building, though it is almost completely devoid of furniture. Some rooms on this level has windows, though most of them have been completely blacked out. Any windows that have not been blacked out are considered traps and should be avoided at all costs. Class 0 1 ENT Level 5 — Terror Hotel Level 5 is a hotel complex, with many rooms and halls. This level itself seems to have been constructed in the 1930s, with furniture dating back to 1920. Class 4 4 ENT Level Fun M.E.G. Notice: The information on this level has been removed as it has been vandalized by the Partygoers. They have breached the credential system and any information here should not be trusted. Class 3 2 ENT Level 37 — The Poolrooms The Poolrooms are an expansive complex of interconnected rooms and corridors slightly submerged in undulating, lukewarm water. Each area of the level varies greatly in size and structure, ranging from uniform pools and hallways to more open, abnormally-shaped areas. Class 1 2 ENT Level ! — Run For Your Life! Run for your Life! is fairly uncomplicated in appearance and layout, though that does not take away from the ever-present danger here. The level consists entirely of one long, narrow hallway reminiscent of hallways commonly found inside modern hospitals. Class 5 2 ENT The End The End creates the illusion of escaping the Backrooms. The layout of this level consists of a modern library, with the main area in the center decorated with letters spelling 'the end'. Wanderers who enter this level will experience complete silence, with some areas measuring at -6.2dB. Class 2 1 ENT Level 94 Level 94 appears to be a large town, a floating castle and grass hills, but everything has a grainy effect, as if the place was crafted. The main town is safe, with no Entities during the day. Many survivors have described it as looking like a suburban neighborhood in Ohio. Class 3 3 ENT Level 6 — Lights Out Level 6 is an expansive complex of indeterminate size, made up of metallic walls, brick floors, and complex systems of pipes. The entirety of Level 6 is shrouded in complete darkness; thus far, no light sources have successfully created any light in the level. Due to the level's darkness, it is not possible to take sufficient photographs of it. Class 2 2 ENT Level 7 — Thalassophobia Level 7 is an impossibly large ocean that seems to stretch infinitely in all directions. Despite the absence of fixed light sources within the level, a dim natural light is present across the level. Detailed exploration of this ocean is sparse due to the extreme danger and amount of preparation it requires to navigate. Class 1 1 ENT Level 8 — Cave System Level 8 is made of huge caverns and small cave systems that twist and turn like normal underground systems. Class 4 4 ENT Level 0.11 Level 0.11 looks just like Level 0 but it's extremely rundown and abandoned. The level also has rooms that contain furniture such as chairs and tables. There's also a smaller version of this level in the final layer in Level 55.1 . Class 2 2 ENT Level 9 — Darkened Suburbs Level 9 is a suburban area at night with many houses that vary in design and size. There have been reports of an outbreak in the M.E.G. base located in the level but it cannot be confirmed. Class 5 4 ENT Level 10 Level 10 is an expansive field of wheat that stretches endlessly in all directions. The climate of the level appears to never change from a foggy, dreary sky, with only the occasional gust of wind among the crops to break up the uniformity. Class 2 1 ENT Level 3999 Level 3999 is a seemingly infinite arcade. Not much is known about the level, but there have been rumors of wanderers being able to return to the Frontrooms from there. Class 0 Level 0.2 The M.E.G. Civilization The M.E.G. Civilization resembles Level 0 but it's much smaller and less maze-like. Near the start, there's a map that suggests the red-carpet aesthetic that resembles Level 0.2 is being intentionally built and spread around by M.E.G. Class 1 1 ENT The Snackrooms The Snackrooms are a completely safe level that resembles a bar from the 1600s and contains many supplies for wanderers. Class 0 1 ENT Level !~! — Hotel Chase Level !~! is a hotel with red lights similar to Level ! . The Level consists of many hallways with a carpet, wooden doors and many exit signs hanging from the ceiling. There are also some blue hallways, which appear to be hallways that are blue, and act as safe zones from the Clump that chases you in this Level. Class 5 2 ENT Level 188 Level 188 is a large complex of interior spaces with a courtyard that contains many windows, some of which have open curtains with views of what appear to be other levels. Class 3 2 ENT Level 37.2 — Dark Poolrooms / Expanded Poolrooms Level 37.2 looks mostly like Level 37 but with new locations, darker areas, and much more danger. Class 3 2 ENT Level Fun+ None Class 4 3 ENT Level 52 Level 52 is a infinite school consisting of hallways, cafeterias, and classrooms similar to a school in the frontrooms. Class 5 2 ENT Level 55.1 Level 55.1 Level 55.1 is a large room with a massive vertical tunnel in the center, and an elevator is connected to the tunnel that can be used to access the various layers. The tunnel has three areas that you can access: Level 0.2, Level 0.3, and Level 0.11 . However, these are smaller versions of their respective levels aside from Level 0.2 and Level 0.3. Class 3 SECRET 2 ENT Level You Cheated Level You Cheated is a room with tan walls, a tan floor, and the ceiling is dark pink. The only thing in the room is a ceiling light and a door at the top. The door at the front of the level is not currently possible to unlock, but every so often, knocks will come from it. Class 0 SECRET 2 ENT Overgrowth Overgrowth looks just like Level 0 , however it's very damaged and abandoned. The level has things like beige wallpaper and moist carpet just like Level 0 , graffiti all over the walls, missing ceiling tiles, damaged ceiling tiles, bacteria deposits over the walls and ground, and air with bacteria making it hard to breathe in, which has caused previous Wanderers to pass out.
