Appearance

Level 974 is an old, 2 story abandoned and rundown pink house. This Level is the home of Mr. Kitty.

Objective

Part 1: The Audience

Once you first spawn in, you'll be in the stage room. After a few seconds of being in the level, Mr. Kitty will spawn. He's completely harmless if you're staring at him, but if you look away, he will get closer and closer the longer you look away. Look away for too long and he'll reach you and kill you. In order to escape, you must go around the level and collect 12 Toys and seat them all down in the 12 seats in the stage room.

Part 2: A Dark Truth

Once all 12 Toys are seated, lights will shine down on x's marked on the stage. The number of lights that spawn depends on how many players you are with. To open up the curtains, everyone must stand on each of the lights, and the stage curtains will open up, revealing 4 Wanderers seated down with a projector in the back reading "Sweet dreams little one". When this occurs, a gas will start spraying down from the ceiling as the player falls down and closes their eyes.

Endings

The Pink House

After passing out from the gas, a cutscene plays where the player wakes up in a much cleaner and normal looking Level 974 when Mr. Kitty opens up the door and starts teleporting all over the place. You will then wake up in the Bunker.

Trivia

  • Level 974's development started in August of 2022, and released on October 23rd, 2025 in Version 1.0 3 years after starting development, making it the Level that has taken the most time to make.
  • Fancy mentioned in his 11th Devlog how this Level was initially one room. This could have actually not been the case however because behind Mr. Kitty standing in the door in the old appearance, you could see hallways.
  • Initially, the appearance of this Level was resemblant of the Level in the original lore with everything being pink and not rundown, however in order to prevent confusion from regular players who do not know about the Backrooms lore who would be really confused as to why they're hours into a horror game and suddenly enter in a bright pink house with plushies, Fancy decided to redesign the entire Level to have it be a rundown multifloored house instead.
  • The original room the Level was supposed to be appears different than how it initially did in Fancy's 11th Devlog.
    • This bedroom was used for The Pink House Ending.
  • This Level was originally planned to be a part of the Wrong Door path, but Fancy didn't add it to not confuse players unfamiliar with the Backrooms lore on the former appearance of the Level.
