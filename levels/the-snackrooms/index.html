Appearance

The Snackrooms is a small bar from the 1600's with many Items for you to stock up on. There is a Typewriter on the front desk. Most of the furniture is wooden with some designs carved into the wood, as well as some chairs, couches and tables. The Level is one room and serves as a transition point to the next Level.

Objective

Once you first spawn in, exit the Elevator and enter the Level. After stepping out of the Elevator for a bit, it will fall (only if there is no one inside). You can jump in the hole that it fell into to die, and this is the only way you can die in this Level. In order to escape, look at the Note and type in the keys as shown on the Note in the Typewriter on the desk, and then the exit door will open. It leads to Level !~!. The code on the Typewriter is 3752.

Entities

Entrances & Exits

Entrances

  • Turn on all the Electricity Boxes in Level 0.2 to close the Bone Thief Gate and run to the Elevator on the other side to get here.

Exits

  • Type the keys on the Typewriter that you see on the Note to access the exit doors, which lead to Level !~!.

Trivia

  • This Level has the greatest number of Items in a single room.
  • Every now and then, knocking will come from the exit door. The knocking will continue until it is opened.
  • The only purpose this Level serves is to be a room where you can stock up on Items for the more dangerous Levels.
  • This Level is the only Level in Part 4 to be made in Fancy's 8th Escape the Backrooms Devlog.
