Level Fun+ Guide

Instructions

Starting room

This level has a main room that has the exit and many items on the tables to keep yourself sane when in the other 4 sectors. As such, you can enter these 4 areas in any order you wish. So what you should do first is grab items and do each sector 1 by 1.

The Sectors you can enter include:

  • The Party Sector (the bright blue hallway)
  • The Playhouse Sector (the pink hallway)
  • The Arcade Sector (the red hallway)
  • The Funhouse Sector (The dark blue hallway)

NOTE: When you complete a sector it will be closed off so you do not get lost trying to find a key you already have. Every Sector also has a Note to tell you your objective as well.

The Party Sector

This Sector is a massive area with a bunch of walls, tables and chairs, and a party room in the middle containing tables, chairs, and a different wall texture. Your goal here is to go around and pop all of the Balloons scattered around because one of them will contain the Yellow Key. However, the Balloon the Yellow Key will be in is different every time. The sector does have 3 Partygoers roaming around, so keep an eye out for them.

The Playhouse Sector

This Sector starts off with rooms similar to the original Level Fun, just much smaller. Eventually, you can find a room which is a big square room with balloons everywhere, and a hallway that connects to 3 rooms. Your goal in this sector is to find a tape in one of the 3 rooms. The Tape has only 1 spawn for every room though, so it shouldn't be too hard to find.

Once you have the tape, go back to the room with the balloons and insert the tape into the TV. The Video shows a balloon icon, and then the color green will flash. You must then simply pop a green balloon. Then, it will do it again, but after green it will show yellow. Then, you have to pop a green and then yellow balloon. Repeat this game until all the balloons are popped.

NOTE: If you get a color wrong, all of the balloons will respawn and you will have to start over.

Once all of the Balloons are popped, the secured vault door will open, and inside is a room with a wooden table. On the wooden table is the green key.

The Arcade Sector

This Sector is a neon-type area with a area that features a bunch of golf courses, and also some tables that contain Glowsticks as well as a Bowling Alley. In the Golf Area, there are 4 Glowstick tables, and each one has a different color. The Colors Include Green, Blue, Red, and Yellow. You will need one of every color as well as an extra one for navigation. Then, go up the stairs and enter the dark area.

Get out one of the Glowsticks to bright up the place, and then you must go around the darkness and find 4 different symbols. 3 of the Symbols are on the walls in the halls, and one of them is in a closet in one of the rooms. The Symbols Include Balloons, a Present, an Eye, and a Rainbow. The Rainbow is the one stored in the closet. Once you find a Symbol, you must place down the correct Glowstick right below it. When you are holding the correct Glowstick, the Symbol will light up entirely instead of partially.

The Balloons need a green Glowstick, the Present will need a red one, the Eye will need a yellow one, and the rainbow will need a blue one. There is a Partygoer roaming around the dark area though, so keep an eye out for it. It has poor eyesight because of the darkness so you will see it before it sees you. Hide under a table or exit that area to avoid being killed.

Once all of the Glowsticks are placed down, you can leave the dark area and go to the locked door which is now open. Inside will be the Blue Key.

The Funhouse Sector

This Sector is a cluster of rooms with different fun children activities in them like Trampolines, a Rock Climbing Wall, a Bounce House and more. The Room you start off in has a locked blue door. On the roof of this classroom lies a Party Host. In order to move on, you must kill it. In order to kill it, grab a Firework from a table and throw it at the Party Host. Then, the blue door will be unlocked.

NOTE: You can kill yourself and teammates if you get caught in the explosion. So throw them from a safe distance to avoid being killed or having your teammate(s) be killed. It will also explode automatically when it makes physical contact with the Party Host.

You will then find yourself in a much bigger room with a wooden pathway as well as other segments. The next Party Host is also above the doors. Every now and then, Partygoers will come out of the doors because of the Party Hosts (even if none currently see you). They'll come out which will force you and all players to hide under a table until they run away.

Once you kill that Party Host, the next room has a rock climbing wall in the middle, and the Party Host is on the top right of the room. Once again, beware of the Partygoers. Once you kill that Party Host, you must go into the room on the right side of the room first. This room has the Party Host above a box with a bunch of presents, and letters reading "HAPPY BIRTHDAY".

NOTE: The Partygoers will go into any rooms even if a door is closed to get a player. Remember to hide under a table until they leave.

After killing that Party Host, you can go into the room on the left side. This room is a ballpit with balloons on the wall. Once again, the Party Host is on the ceiling. Kill it, and then you can move onto the next room. The doors to proceed on will be at the top of the room, and this next area is the hardest in this Sector.

It features a pathway to walk on, and a ballpit. The pathway will lead to the Party Host right above the steel door. After killing that Party Host, you can go up a slope that leads down into an area that doesn't have a ballpit, but does have a trampoline and structural support. The Party Host here is quite high, because it's on the ceiling. So you will have to time your throw while jumping to hit it. Make sure to keep an eye on the steel door as well since that is where the Partygoers will come from when summoned. Kill the Party Host, and a room on the bottom right of this area will unlock that has the Red Key.

NOTE: Killing all the Party Hosts will make the sector safe to traverse.

Opening the Gate

Once you have all 4 Keys, you can go back to the main area and go up to a Key Machine. In the Key Machine, you must move around the "arm" in the Machine and go to the Key hole that corresponds with the color of the Key it currently has out. Use, WASD to move it in the 4 direction and Left Click to insert the key into the hole. Once that is done, insert the Key into the Hole, and another Key will pop out and the previous one will fall down. Now, you must go to the Key Hole that corresponds with that Key. Repeat until all 4 keys have been inserted into the machine. However, after using the last Key, the Exit Gate nearby will open and you can get to Level 52.
