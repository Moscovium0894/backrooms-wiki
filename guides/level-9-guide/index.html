Level 9 Guide

This level is split into 2 sections. The neighborhood and the abandoned M.E.G. base, which will be explained in different guides. This will be just for Level 9 itself.

Instructions (The Neighborhood)

You first need to approach the metal gate in front of the abandoned M.E.G. base and find a virtual map that shows all the houses. You will see a few houses that are flashing red. These houses are the houses that you need to go to. Start finding these houses and enter them one by one. Your main goal is to activate something on the computers. There is also a Neighborhood Watch that is extremely fast that roams around the roads of the level, which you need to be careful for. If it chases you, quickly run into a house and close the door.

Inside the houses, you need to also watch out for Wretches. There are locks behind nearly every door, which you can use so the wretch cannot open them. You need to repeat that with each house until you get all 3 computers done. After that, you need to go to the M.E.G. base and the door will unlock. Then head inside and follow the arrow on the wall into the basement.

After completing the Abandoned Outpost, head to the back gate and use the keycard you got to open it using the terminal and head into Level 10.

NOTE: The map image below has the houses marked that can have them. It is recommended that you do any house that you get that is marked as "safe" since that is a free computer you can activate with no worry of a Wretch being inside that house. You could get good luck and have all 3 houses be safe or be unlucky and have all 3 be dangerous. Most of the time you will get 1 house safe however.
