Abandoned Outpost Guide

Instructions

Get the Concentrate

Once you first spawn in, you must proceed on forward to get to the Base. Once in, you'll first find yourself in a room with a cage. When entering this area, a movie will play explaining how to turn a Wretch back to normal. Once it's done, you must go around the place and find 4 Bottles of Almond Concentrate. There are Lockers scattered around the place that you must hide in if the Wretch spots you.

Collect the Keycard

Once you have all the Bottles of Concentrate, put them all into the machine first. (Hold one out in your hand then press F to put it in). Once all 4 are inside lure the Wretch to the cage, and it will close. Then, push the emergency shower forward until you can't move it anymore. Turn it on, and the Wretch will be reverted back into a human. Collect the Keycard, and you can leave the Outpost back to Level 9 so that you can use it to get to Level 10.
