Sanity

Sanity is a mechanic in Escape the Backrooms. It involves slowly going insane.

How it Works

Each player has a Sanity bar that is viewable in the Inventory. There is a brain on the bar, and it gets more deteriorated the lower your Sanity gets. Sanity will go down over time, draining by a set percentage every 5 seconds, and in order to restore Sanity, you can drink Almond Water or Juice or eat an Energy Bar or Moth Jelly. Once your Sanity is nearly gone, the player will hallucinate Smilers around you for a second. The player blinks, making the hallucination disappear, and say "What happened? I must be low on sanity...". If you don't restore your Sanity at this point, the player hallucinates another time, but the Smilers won't go away and instead just increase, and then you will die from going insane.

Sanity drains in:

Sanity drain occurs every 5 seconds and goes down by a set percentage. This percentage is multiplied by 1.5x in Nightmare.

  • Level 1 - 0.5%
  • Level 3 - 1%
  • Level 5
    • The Main Hall - 1.5%
    • The Hotel - 1.75%
    • The Boiler Room - 1%
  • Level Fun - 2%
  • Level ! - 1%
  • The End - 1%
  • Level 94 (Town area) - 2.25%
  • Level 6 - 1.5%
  • Level 7 - 1.75%
    • Sanity doesn't drain while in a boat.
  • Level 8 - 1.37%
  • Level 9 - 2.5%
  • Level 10 - 1%
  • Level 0.2 - 0.3%
  • Level 188 - 1%
  • Level 37.2 - 1%
    • Since Sanity restores in the bright area, this is usually not noticeable.
  • Level Fun+ (Sectors) - 0.5%
  • Level 52 - 1%
  • Level 55.1 - 0.5%
  • Overgrowth - 0.75%

Tips & Advice

  • The speed of Sanity drain is level dependent. It goes down faster in levels like Level 94 or Level 9, however, in levels like Level 0 or Level 4, it does not drain at all. Level 37 and its Expanded Version will also restore Sanity.
  • Sanity drops in Level 37.2, but once you go to the Expanded Poolrooms, it will be instantly restored.
  • Almond Water restores 50% of your Sanity.
  • Juice restores 25% of your Sanity.
  • Energy Bar restores 25% of your Sanity.
  • Moth Jelly restores 35% of your Sanity.

Difficulty Changes

  • Sanity cannot drain in Easy.
  • Sanity drains at a normal speed in Normal and Hard.
  • Sanity will drain 1.5x faster in Nightmare.
