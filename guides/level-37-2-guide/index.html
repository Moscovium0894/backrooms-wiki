Level 37.2 Guide

Instructions

You will be switching back and forth between the new Level 37 area (Expanded Poolrooms) and the multiple Level 37.2 areas (Dark Poolrooms). As such, this will be done in order.

Expanded Poolrooms 1

Once you first spawn in, you will find yourself in a big room with windows, and a hole that you must crouch to enter so you can proceed onto the rest of the Level. When you enter the next area, there will be a Pool that has the Hydrolitis Plague. Then, you will be able to find a Floatie Boat. Get in the boat, and start travelling. Shortly in, the path will have no more Water, which means you will have to take a Ladder to go up to the Valves. There are 4 Valves, and all you need to do is turn them all on. They are on the right side as well. Once you turn on all the Valves, the path will fill with Water, and you will be able to continue on.

These Valves are on a timer. Like the Timed Levers, the timer will depend on how many players are alive. You will then go down a very fast Water path. In the next area, the Water will be too low to continue. There will be a nearby Ladder, and when you get up, the entrance to the first Dark Poolrooms will be accessible. There will be 3 Ladders, and you can take any one of them, and you will arrive in the first Dark Poolrooms Area.

Dark Poolrooms 1

In the Dark Poolrooms, you must go around and turn 4 Valves in order to fill up a Tank with Water. Just make sure you keep an eye out for the Clump in the Level. If he spots you, just hide in the Water and he will leave you alone. Once all of the Valves are turned, you can go back to the Expanded Poolrooms. If you're not sure you got them all, check the Water Tank to see if it's full.

Expanded Poolrooms 2

When you get back to the Expanded Poolrooms, the Water will be filled, and you will be able to get back in the Floatie Boat and continue on. However, shortly into travelling, you run into another obstacle, which is a Giant Pit. You must then once again go up the Ladder on the left or right and turn all the Valves (this time there's 4 on the left and the right) there's also a Bridge that will let you easily get to the other side. However, if you run on it or if too many players are on it at the same time, it will break so be careful. If it does break don't worry because there is a ladder that leads to a back area that connects them.

Once all the Valves are turned, the Pit will be filled and you can get back in the Boat and continue your travelling. However, soon enough, there will be another obstacle. The Path cuts off, so you must get the Boat on the other side. There will be another Ladder that you must go up and the entrance to the second Dark Poolrooms Area will be there.

Dark Poolrooms 2

The Second Dark Poolrooms Area is the exact same thing as the first one, but with a different Map Layout. It's also the easiest. However, there is some Hydrolitis Plague in the Level in a parkour-like area. Turn all 3 Valves, then leave.

Expanded Poolrooms 3

After leaving the second Dark Poolrooms Area, go through the Triangular entrance to get to the next part of The Expanded Poolrooms. There will be a hole, and inside is the Floatie Boat. After travelling for a bit, you will run into yet again another obstacle. The Water won't be high enough to proceed. So you will have to take the nearby Ladder, and then you will have to take the stairs and then you will arrive at the above part of this area. In this above part, the entrance to the third and final Dark Poolrooms will be there.

Dark Poolrooms 3

You know how it works. Same thing as last time, but just a different Map layout. Except this time there are 7 Valves instead of 4. Turn all 7, then leave.

Expanded Poolrooms 4

Once you return to the Expanded Poolrooms, the Water will be high enough to continue, however they have stairs at the top. At this point, you will no longer need the Boat, so just get on the Stairs and on the other side is a Pool with 3 Entrances to a Big Area. This Big Area has a bunch of Colored Slides with a Staircase leading up to them.

Go up the Staircase and at the top is a room with all 4 of the slides. Enter one of the Slides (doesn't matter which one) and you will find yourself in Level Fun+.
