Level 8 Guide

When entering the Level, gather supplies from the table and proceed forward into the decontamination room.

From here, crouch so you don't upset the Female Death Moths and go left until you find the next decontamination room.

Keep doing this again and go left until you find the bridge. There is a Note on it. From here you either walk or crouch walk across the bridge. If on multiplayer, 1 player at a time. Sprinting or more than 1 player on it will cause the bridge to collapse and will likely result in death.

If this happens there is an alternate path to the right side of the bridge that goes upwards into a small hole.

It is recommended you cross the bridge so you can get more items.

For the final area it isn't Female Death Moths you have to deal with but, Skin-Stealers instead.

Hug the right wall until you find the decontamination room, if you see a flashlight hide around a corner and pay attention to where it is pointing since that is where the Skin-Stealer is looking.

If caught try and run around the area and make a dash for the exit or back to the entrance IF you crossed the bridge and turn the valve to be saved from it.

After the 3rd area, head left and up the ladder to head to a new area in Level 0.
