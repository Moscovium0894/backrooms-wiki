Level 55.1 Guide

Instructions

When you spawn in there are tables nearby with many items to use so grab some to keep yourself sane. Be sure to take a flashlight. Proceed forward to the middle of the room and you'll see tables with computers, cameras, and a note. When you pick up the camera and hold it out you will see a photo on it (this is what you must take a picture of). If on Multiplayer your first image will be to take a photo of another player (or multiple). After this first image or if on Singleplayer take the elevator down.

The lever will switch between going up and down. Between it will be to stop it entirely. So remember this since you can use it to escape from any entities you will encounter.

The first layer resembles Level 0.2, your first image if on Singleplayer is to take a picture of the Graffiti on the wall, then search around for an opening into Level 0 and take a picture of that.

NOTE: For the photos taken you might need to get closer than you think for it to count, you will know if it counted if the photo on the camera changes to the next one you need to take.

After this layer head down into the 2nd layer that resembles Level 0.3. Down here you will need to locate a Van to take a picture of it. Around the layer is some Lockers you can hide in. You can see where this is going, your next picture is to take one of the Bacteria. Which is dangerous since it blends into the darkness. This is 1 reason why I stated to take a flashlight with you.

After this layer head down into the final one, it resembles Level 0.11. Down here is your final 2 pictures. There are many Smilers down here so use the flashlight to kill them. They do not respawn so wait by the elevator so they come to you and they will die 1 by 1. Once you don't see anymore Smilers you only have to worry about a Skin-Stealer.

Look around the layer for some Moai Statues to take a photo of. Your final picture is of the Skin-Stealer itself. Due note that you do NOT need it to be in its true form. If it's disguised and you sneak up to take a photo it will still count. There are some lockers to hide in, as for the previous floor.

After you take this final photo a hatch above the elevator will open up. Take the elevator all the way up and enter this hidden layer. It is an overgrown area and there is a door you can open that leads into a bright pit. Jump down and you will get 1 of the game's endings (Currently it is the ONLY ending).
