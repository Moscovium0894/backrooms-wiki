Appearance

Partygoers are yellow humanoid creatures with blood being easily noticeable on the mouth, hands, and legs. They hold Balloons and they stay in the same position, but if they spot you they will start chasing you. In Level Fun+, they walk around the place and don't just stand in one place.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level Fun
  • Level !
  • Level Fun+
  • Level 52

Behavior

Level Fun

They will stay standing still looking in a certain direction. If you get too close to one, or one of the Partygoers spots you, all of the nearest Partygoers (along with the one you got too close to) will chase you down. At that point, your only hope is to either hide underneath a Table or run all the way to the exit without getting caught. After you hide, all the Partygoers will return to their original spots. You can also get their attention by popping a bunch of Balloons on a Table.

Level !

In Level !, there is a pack of 6 Partygoers that will be in front of The Giant Smiler in the back, and they simply just chase you in this Level. When the player runs through the side hall, one of the partygoers will follow you down the hall and rejoin the group when you exit the hall.

Level Fun+

The Party & Arcade Sector

In the Party Sector in Level Fun+, 3 Partygoers will walk around the area. If one spots you, it will start chasing you. Simply just hide underneath a Table to avoid it. In the dark area of the Arcade Sector, there will only be one Partygoer, and he won't be able to spot you unless you get too close.

The Funhouse Sector

In the Funhouse Sector in Level Fun+, in every room there will be steel doors. If the Party Host in the room spots you or after some time, all of the Partygoers will leave the room and start chasing the player. Simply hide underneath a Table and they will retreat back to the room.

Level 52

In Level 52, you must follow the Partypooper and if he crouches, you must too because it means a Partygoer is nearby. The Partygoers won't move, and if you don't crouch, the Partygoers will come after you and will kill the Partypooper first if you are farther from him.

Talking

Partygoers have the ability to talk, and you can hear them when in certain parts of Level Fun. Even if there aren't actually any of them around, you can still hear them, for example at the locked Blue Door or if you get all the Partygoers stuck at the Fun Room and you go back into the area where you heard it originally you will still hear it even though the Partygoers aren't there. They have 5 Lines, and they say their Lines in a specific order.

  • It's a party, come to play.
  • You're missing out on the party.
  • We can have so much fun.
  • We have a cake, come try it.
  • There is arcade games.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Crouch when trying to get around one.
  • If spotted, run for the nearest Table and crouch underneath it.
  • Peek around a corner before going.
  • Distract them, if you are playing in Multiplayer.

Don'ts

  • Walk/sprint behind or near it.
  • Pop a Balloon Cluster with no intention of getting under a Table.
  • Trigger a chase without knowing where the nearest Table is.
  • Touch them while standing up.
  • Run around a corner before peeking.

Tips & Advice

As long as one's not facing you, it won't come after you. If it's impossible to go around without it seeing you, get them to chase you by popping a Balloon or making it see you, then hide under a Table. This will most likely turn it around.

Trivia

  • The Partygoers originally had a different Model, and they were a lot more friendly looking compared to how they look now. It was changed in Part 2.
  • Even though the old Partygoers were removed in Part 2, an old Partygoer can be seen on the Party Sector Note in Level Fun+. This is most likely because this Note was originally meant for a different purpose before Level Fun+ was added. (it was in the game files when Part 2 released)
  • While crouching, you can actually touch the back of a Partygoer without getting killed. This feature was added in Part 2.
  • Partygoers are responsible for the near extinction of Partypoopers due to The Fun War.
