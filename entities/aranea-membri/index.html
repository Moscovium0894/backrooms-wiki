Appearance

The Aranea Membri is a massive, spider-like creature with a skull of an unknown animal for a head. It has 4 large legs that gives it a high level of speed.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 188

Behavior

The Aranea Membri will roam around the hotel floor in Level 188. If it spots a player, it will start to chase them down. It is faster than a player, meaning the only way to hide from it is by entering a hotel room, or enter one of the rooms at the corners of the hotel. It can also sometimes come out of Vents on the ceiling when you approach them, in which it will immediately start chasing you once it gets out of the Vent.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Run as fast as you can away from it into a hotel room if seen.
  • Keep nearby doors open so you can have an easier and faster time hiding.
  • Peek around a corner before going towards it.
  • Avoid being in the hallways for too long.
  • Lean around TV's by pressing Q or E if you're pushing one to see in front of you, in case the Aranea Membri is there.

Don'ts

  • Roam hallways for long periods of time.
  • Try to hide in one of the smaller rooms.
  • Take too long to enter a room due to its high speed.
  • Run around a corner if you don't know where it is.

Trivia

  • The Aranea Membri in the game is a female, which is because in the Backrooms lore, male Aranea Membri have antlers on their heads like deer.
  • The Aranea Membri can be seen in the game's Steam artwork and in the thumbnail of Fancy's 3rd Devlog from 2022, however it didn't appear in the game until Part 4 in 2024.
  • This entity was created by a Youtuber named Frag 2 and not by the Backrooms Wikidot and Fandom. It was later added on the Backrooms Fandom Wiki with an explanation that it was made by Frag 2 at the top of its page.
