Appearance

Smilers are floating smiling faces with sharp teeth with dark shadowy fog surrounding their bodies. Smilers are mysterious and shadow-like entities that are typically seen in pitch black places where only their smiling faces can be seen. They also seem to be afraid of or weak to light likely due to their dark nature, because they can be killed with certain light-related tools.

Locations

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 1 - Habitable Zone
  • Level 2 - Pipe Dreams
  • Level ! - Run For Your Life!
  • Level 0.2 (in Level 0.1)
  • Level !~! - Hotel Chase
  • Level 55.1 (in the Level 0.11 layer)

Behavior

Level 1

In the garage on the 4th floor in Level 1, every now and then the lights flicker, and shortly after the flicker, they completely turn off. When the lights are off, Smilers will spawn everywhere, and if you aren't in the halls which are the only area with lights when the lights turn off, all of the Smilers within the darkness will start to chase players down, in which they must get back to the light to prevent the Smilers from reaching them. Act quick however, because they are faster than a Wanderer.

Chasing

In Level 2 and Level !, at certain points in each level, there are Smilers that will chase you or your teammates down. The Smiler in Level 2 will chase you if you're travelling in either direction of the hallway once your almost out of the darkness. It will also break any active light it goes underneath. The Smiler in Level ! will chase you once all your teammates enter the hallway.

Roaming

In the Level 0.1 area in Level 0.2 and in the Level 0.11 layer of Level 55.1, there are tons of Smilers that roam around aimlessly, and the ones in Level 0.1 break any light they go underneath temporarily. They will chase a Wanderer if they see one, and they can be fended off using a Flashlight or a Camera if you're in Level 55.1 by shining their lights at the Smiler.

Level !~!

When running through the dangerous hotel of Level !~!, be sure to take the right way, because if you take the wrong way, a Smiler will come out of that wrong hallway and chase you down. If it's triggered, there is no escaping it.

Do's & Don'ts

Level 1

Do's

  • Try to stay in the light.
  • Run when the lights turn on.

Don'ts

  • Stay or go into the area while the lights are turned off.
  • Try to outrun the Smilers, they are faster than you.

Chasing

Do's

  • Run without looking back.
  • Run the entire time.
  • Hop if you are more advanced.

Don'ts

  • Stop running while being chased.

Level !~!

Do's

  • Enter the correct hallways.

Don'ts

  • Enter the wrong hallways.
  • Try to run back to the correct hallway, it will always reach you because it's also faster than you.

Roaming

Do's

  • Turn on a Flashlight or a Camera if in Level 55.1 and shine it at them.
  • Keep your distance when turning on a Flashlight to make sure they don't kill you before they do.

Don'ts

  • Let them get too close to you.
  • Run around a corner before peeking or slowly passing it.

Update History

Trivia

  • Smilers are the most common entity in Escape the Backrooms, being in 6 total levels.
  • The Smilers in Level 1 are fast enough that a player usually has less than half a second to get into the hallways before they catch up.
  • Smilers were originally big spider-like being with spiky limbs that helped them stand. This was changed to just be a floating face in Part 2. The spider Smiler still existed in Level 2 (and Level ! with low graphics on) for some time until in Version 1.0, all Smilers were completely replaced with their current fog floating face bodies.
  • There used to be a smiler variant in Level 1 known as the Ghost Smiler. It was removed in Part 2.
  • Smilers are replacements of Lighthead.
  • When Fancy first showed what a Smiler looked like out of the dark, he showed a tiny Smiler before promptly kicking it to a wall. After this, he said that he made them a bit bigger. In Fancy's 4th Devlog, the tiny Smiler returned while Fancy was trying to figure out the situation of whether to remove the bodies of Smilers and make them floating faces when he decided to shoot the tiny smiler.
