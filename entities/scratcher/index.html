Appearance

The Scratcher is a skinny gray humanoid with red glowing eyes. It crawls on all fours and has disproportionately large hands and legs. The Scratcher also lacks facial features.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • The End

Behavior

The Scratcher will roam around The End randomly. It also doesn't have eyes, making it Blind. If you make noise around it, it will figure out you're there and become faster than you. It will start chasing you and the only thing you can do at that point is hide underneath one of the 2 Tables in the Level. After a few Seconds of being underneath the Table, it will go away. You can also use a Fire Alarm to distract it. There are 2 in the Level, and once turning one on, the Scratcher will go towards the noise. Once it makes it to the Fire Alarm, it will go back to roaming around The End. Once you collect all of the Tapes and put them into the Computer, the Scratcher will ignore you, and after a few Seconds, it will dissappear and The Orb will spawn.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Stay far away from it.
  • Keep an eye on it at all times so you know where it's at.
  • Crouch and get near a wall if it gets close.
  • Hide underneath a Table if it's after you.
  • Use Fire Alarms to distract it.
  • Be directly in the corner when hiding.

Don'ts

  • Walk or Run near it.
  • Look away for long enough, as it can walk into you or near you.
  • If using in-game voice chat, do not talk anywhere near it.

Tips & Advice

Since the Scratcher is Blind, make sure to be stealthy and do not pick Tapes if it is near. Remember to keep your distance from it. The Scratcher rarely goes into Corners, so they are a good place to be. If the Scratcher starts chasing you, don't be afraid to Run (if it's safe to Run) and get under a Table. Make sure to be quick since The Scratcher is faster than you if it hears you.

Trivia

  • On the Backrooms Fandom Wiki, the Scratcher doesn't exist in The End.
  • In the Game's Release, the Scratcher had a different Model which was nearly the same but it was not twitchy and was completely black. The Model was changed in Part 2.
