Appearance

Wanderers are humans that are trapped in the Backrooms who are always seen wearing a hazmat suit, the design of which can be changed in the Character Select screen. All Wanderers are about 1.8m tall (~5'10.87ft). In the game, you play as a Wanderer, whom the identity of is currently unknown. Other Wanderers have been mentioned and even seen in some parts of the game, which don't wear the same hazmat suits as players, but instead wear a similar looking one that appears to be a remodeled design.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 0 (cutscene)
  • Every Level (players)
  • The Bunker (empty hazmat suits)
  • Overgrowth (known as "Explorers")
  • Level 974
  • Abandoned Outpost

Trivia

  • 2 redesigned Hazmat suits can be found in the Bunker behind glass in a hallway.
  • If a player has a Firework or a Knife, it can be used to kill other players.
  • The reason Wanderers are considered Entities is because of Entity 1 in the Backrooms Wikidot lore being human beings.
  • Fancy revealed in the 6th Devlog that the Wanderer you play as is a 67-year-old man from Ohio named Rupert. This might not be true anymore since the Wanderer you play as talks in the Empty City Ending and doesn't sound as old as Rupert.
  • The Wanderer model was first added when Multiplayer was added on the game's Release.
  • Before Multiplayer was made, the Wanderer was just floating hands.
  • The other Wanderer model was previously supposed to replace the player model as well, but due to players not liking the new model, the current player model was made instead.
  • The Wanderer model has gone through 4 redesigns to fix the original model.
