Unknown N/A

"So the next level i'm going to add is Le-Hold up what's this?" The topic of this page does not have an official name! So, to make sure the page at least has a title, a conjectural title is in place until an official name is revealed.

Appearance

The entity appears to be a dark, giant human-sized creature with glowing blue eyes and something on its head. It also has two really long fingers on its hand.

Location

It's unknown for certain where the entity will be, although it will likely be in the new level Fancy mentioned when it appeared considering this entity has never been seen before, and it wouldn't appear for no reason at that moment. It might also be in Level 5 considering that's where it appeared at.

Trivia

Speculation

  • There are a couple possibilities on what this entity is:
    • The first and strongest possibility is The Beast of Level 5, which has a few points:
      • Fancy was in Level 5 where it appeared, the main level the beast resides in on the Backrooms Wikidot.
      • Its head kind of looks like the head of the beast when you look at just the head.
      • It looks like it might be wearing a suit or some sort of clothing when looking at the back of it.
      • If the beast is the entity, then the level Fancy was saying is his favorite in the update might be Level 5.1, since if it's being added to the game, it would make sense that that's what would lead to the beast's addition.
        • To add to the level being Level 5.1, it doesn't sound unrealistic for a level like Level 5.1 to be Fancy's favorite of the new levels.
    • The second biggest possibility is a Strangler due to a few points and the entity and Stranglers both sharing some features:
      • Both creatures have large beaks, snakelike appendages, and are hunched over.
      • If the Strangler is the entity, then the level Fancy was saying is his favorite in the update might be Level 58.1, since that is the main level Stranglers reside in.
      • One thing that goes against the possibility of it being a Strangler is that it and its parent level is archived outside of the Backrooms Wikidot where it originates, as entity 47 on the Wikidot is now an entity known as Teddy, so it's unclear if Fancy would end up finding the entity because everything within the game seems to come from the Backrooms Wikidot and Fandom wiki.
    • The third possibility is an updated version of Jerry, which has some points for it.
      • It somewhat resembles a bird with a beak and a tongue, although it's hard to tell exactly what it is.
      • If Jerry was changed to work with the game, this entity is probably how it would be done.
      • Its eye color is blue just like Jerry's feathers, although its eyes in Fancy's 7th Escape the Backrooms devlog were red.
      • If Jerry is the entity, then the level Fancy was saying is his favorite in the update might be Level 274, since that is the main level Jerry resides in.
