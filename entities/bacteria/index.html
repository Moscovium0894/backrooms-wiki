Appearance

Bacterias appear to be a skeletal creature made up of thin lines of an unknown substance. A chunky head with holes in it is attached to the humanoid body, and their skin is rough and dingy.

Location

This entity can be found in:

  • Level 0
  • Overgrowth
  • Level 0.11
  • Level 55.1

Behavior

Level 0

After you spend 3 minutes on this Level, the Bacteria will spawn. It will spawn near the player, mimicking knocking sounds to attempt to lure the player in. Once it spots you, it will continuously chase you down until it loses sight of you for some time. If you want to evade it, you must find a dead end, which will then cause the Bacteria to respawn somewhere else.

Roaming

Roaming Bacteria are found in every other level that they are in other than Level 0. If a roaming Bacteria spots you, it will immediately chase you down at a slightly faster speed than on the other Levels, being faster than a Wanderer so running away won't do you any good. If you want to survive, hide in a Vent or a room if in Level 0.11, hide underneath an open hole if in Overgrowth, and hide in a Locker if in Level 55.1 and it won't be able to get you, eventually giving up and returning to its aimless roaming. The one in Level 55.1 specifically appears in the Level 0.3 layer and is slower than the one in Level 0.

Do's & Don'ts

Level 0

Do's

  • Turn corners as much as you can to break line of sight.
  • Try locating the nearest exit.
  • Run away and don't look back until you can't hear it anymore.

Don'ts

  • Run away in a straight line.
  • Follow knocking sounds.

Level 0.11

Do's

  • Keep doors open to have a hiding spot ready in case you're spotted.
  • Find and hide in Vents to avoid it.
  • Peek around corners to avoid accidentally bumping into it.

Don'ts

  • Attempt to outrun the Bacteria, as it is faster than you in Level 0.11.
  • Blindly run around a corner without peeking first.
  • Stay near the vents opening, as it might reach you there.
  • Try attacking it with the Chainsaw. This will result in death since the Chainsaw cannot be used as a weapon.

Level 55.1

Do's

  • Hide in a Locker if you aren't ready to take its picture.
  • Keep in mind of where the nearest Locker is and where the Elevator is at.
  • Get on the elevator and raise it upward so it cannot reach you.

Don'ts

  • Go into a dark area without a Flashlight.
  • Run around carelessly, as you might accidentally run into it.
  • Wait for it to come, because that takes a lot of time.

Trivia

  • If you have the VHS effect on, the Bacteria will cause it to glitch and produce static.
  • Bacterias originate from the level Overgrowth where they seem to come from large blobs of bacteria scattered around the level. This seems to be indicating that Bacteria are sentient bacteria. This is likely also why they are so populous in the level.
  • In previous versions of the Game, the static gain was a lot heavier when looking at it.
  • The name "Bacteria" originates from Kane Pixel's Ko-fi gallery in which an image of the Lifeform is titled "bacteria".
  • In Part 4, the Bacteria's AI was meddled with a little bit, which caused it to not spawn on Level 0, even after three minutes passed. This seems to be fixed in Version 1.0.
  • The main artwork for the game has the shadow of the Bacteria, but the silhouette matches the Lifeform from Kane Pixel's first Backrooms video, and potentially specifically from the thumbnail.
  • The Bacteria is presumably made up of Mutated Hay Bacillus since the Lifeform from Kane Pixels Backrooms series (where the Lifeform originates from) is made up of it in the series, although it's uncertain if this something thought up for the game since Escape the Backrooms doesn't have as much story as Kane's series.
