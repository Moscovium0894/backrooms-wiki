Moai Statue

Unknown Statue

Moai Statues are Large Statues carved out to resemble a human head. The Statues originate from Easter Island.

Sighted in
Level 0.11 Level 55.1 Level 🗿

Appearance

Moai Statues are Large Statues carved out to resemble a human head. The Statues originate from Easter Island.

Location

This Entity was going to appear in:

  • Level 🗿

Behavior

Upon seeing the Statues, they would appear to be harmless but if you would turn your back for even a split second they would crush you.

Trivia

  • The Moai Statues make cameos in Level 55.1 as Objects that you must take a Photo of in the Level 0.11 Layer using the Camera to proceed on. They are harmless in Level 55.1.
  • After some time, Fancy thought the Statues in Level 0.11 looked absolutely ridiculous and just deleted them all.
  • This Entity was originally going to be used for Level 🗿, but Level 🗿 became Level 0.11 and the Statues were moved to Level 0.11 as well.
