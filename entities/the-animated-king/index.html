Appearance

The Animated King is a plastic clown with green hair, red gloves, a purple and yellow clown outfit, and some green clown shoes. It is the Boss Entity of Level 94.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 94

Behavior

When you first find it in the Animated Kingdom in Level 94, it will be standing still on a Podium and won't move. Once you look away for a couple seconds, it will laugh and disappear. A few seconds after it disappears, the lights will turn off, and The Animated King will be after you. It will only attempt to attack wherever you aren't looking and will sneak up behind you. The Animated King is always faster than you, and if it reaches you, it will crush you with its Hammer. If you spot it sneaking up behind you, it will disappear and wait some more time until attacking again.

Tips & Advice

You should always turn around and look for The Animated King because if you stay still, it will come from behind and kill you.

When looking head on at the timer next to the Roller Coaster, right behind you over the fence will be an area, in which when in, The Animated King won't be able to reach you. To get to the other side of the Table, you have to run and jump over the bench next to the Table and inside you can wait safely until the Boss Battle is over.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Turn around all the time and look out for it.
  • Move continuously.
  • Stand in a corner with your FOV high so The Animated King can't get to you.

Don'ts

  • Stay still for long periods of time.
  • Wait more than 100 seconds.

Trivia

  • The Animated King is currently the only Boss Entity in the Game.
  • The Animated King has completely different models for its boss fight and before it.
  • The hostile version of The Animated King has different clothes compared to its normal version.
  • The reason The Animated King is a clown despite being a king is because Fancy couldn't find any reference images of the Animated King.
  • Despite the fact that Fancy couldn't figure out what the Animated King looks like, according to the Backrooms wikidot, The King's true form is a stone statue wielding a sword.
