Appearance

Facelings are humanoid Entities that look like humans however they lack faces and shoes.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 10

Behavior

Stalker Faceling

At the start of Level 10, as you're walking down the road, a Faceling will try to sneak up behind you and get you. In order to make it go back into the Wheat, you must stare at it, and it will run off back into the Wheat. It will continue to attempt to get you until you arrive at the barn, and it doesn't make any sound too, so keep watch for it. If it manages to get to you, it will kidnap you and take you into the Wheat.

Farmer Faceling

As you are turning on the Windmills in Level 10, a Faceling with a Chainsaw will be after you. Once it finds you, it will turn on its Chainsaw and start chasing you. Even if you manage to lose it, it will still be after you.

Tips & Advice

Stalker Faceling

Do's

  • Turn around every few seconds and stare at them until you see them walk towards the fields.
  • Run away so you have more time to look at it before it catches up and grabs you.

Don'ts

  • Keep walking and never turn around.
  • Walk, since you will have less time to turn around and look at it before it grabs you.

Farmer Faceling

Do's

  • Use the startup time on your first encounter to gain more distance on it before it starts chasing.
  • Use Juice to get speed.
  • Get far away until you cannot hear it's Chainsaw, you most likely lost it.

Don'ts

  • Run blindly in a wheat field without knowing the layout, you might just corner yourself.
  • Run towards any Chainsaw noise you hear.

Trivia

  • On the Backrooms Wiki, the facial features of a Faceling are considered as a "decent to downright terrible photoshop job using the delete, smear, and/or blur tool". So Fancy decided to use these tools to create the Faceling textures.
  • Fancy brought up the Faceling in his first ever Escape the Backrooms Devlog, but it wasn't added to the Game until Part 3 (which was in june of 2023).
