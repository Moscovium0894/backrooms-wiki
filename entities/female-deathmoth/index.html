Female Deathmoth

Dangerous Giant Moth

Female Deathmoths are black, white, and gray Moths that are massive in size and have a mark on their back that vaguely resembles a skull.

Survival card

DO

  • Crouch if you're in a room with a Female Deathmoth inside.
  • If spotted, run to the nearest Bug Spray Valve and turn it on.
  • If you hear scuttling, run and turn on the nearest Bug Spray Valve.

DON'T

  • Run near them, it will cause them to chase you.
  • Get them to chase you without knowing where the nearest Bug Spray Valve is.
  • Crouch too close to them.
Sighted in
Level 5 Level 8

Appearance

Female Deathmoths are black, white, and gray Moths that are massive in size and have a mark on their back that vaguely resembles a skull.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 5
  • Level 8

Behavior

Female Deathmoths sit on the wall. If you get near one, it will get off the wall and start chasing you. If it catches you and you are near a Hive, it will take you to the Hive you are closest to. If there are no Hives nearby, it will just kill you right there. You can also shine a Flashlight at one to aggravate it. In order to get past them, you have to crouch by one. If you get spotted, you can also turn a Bug Spray Valve which can only be found in Level 5 and the Female Deathmoth won't cross the Bug Spray. It will just go back to its original spot. If your screen appears to start having a meltdown effect in The Boiler Room in Level 5, that means a Female Deathmoth is nearby. You can use a Thermometer to help you know where they are. If the Temperature is 125 Fahrenheit (51.5 Celsius), it means that a Female Deathmoth is nearby. If the Temperature is 140 Fahrenheit (60 Celsius), it means that the Hive is very close to you, and you should leave immediately. If you are in a Hive, the Temperature will reach it's highest at 788 Fahrenheit (420 Celsius).

Tips & Advice

As long as you crouch, they don't know you're there. If they do see you, make sure to turn on a Bug Spray Valve so they can't get to you.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Crouch if you're in a room with a Female Deathmoth inside.
  • If spotted, run to the nearest Bug Spray Valve and turn it on.
  • If you hear scuttling, run and turn on the nearest Bug Spray Valve.
  • Use a Thermometer to help figure out where a Female Deathmoth is.
  • Eat Moth Jelly or any Item that provides the player with a temporary speed boost to increase your chances of survival if chased.

Don'ts

  • Run near them, it will cause them to chase you.
  • Get them to chase you without knowing where the nearest Bug Spray Valve is.
  • Crouch too close to them.
  • Shine a Flashlight at them in Level 5.

Trivia

  • Despite being Moths, Deathmoths make fly noises instead of Moth noises. This might be because Moths don't make noise aside from their wings flapping.
  • Interestingly, there's an actual moth species named the death moth.
