Appearance

Animations resemble childlike wooden puppets built for kids in the 1930s with red glowing eyes. They spawn during the night to kill those who do not hide in the houses of Level 94. They aren't the smartest entities, because while they know how to break into a house, they can't figure out hiding places.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 94

Behavior

Once the sun starts going down and the night arrives, Animations will spawn. If a player is out in the open during the night, the Animations will start chasing them. They travel in groups and are faster than Wanderers, so escaping them is nearly impossible. In order to avoid them, you have to hide in a house. A house isn't completely safe from Animations, because they can break into houses. To keep them from breaking in, turn off the light using a Light Switch and/or hide underneath the Bed or in the Closet. If they figure out someone's in a house, they will come and knock on the door. At that point, if you're in a house, you only have a couple seconds to hide and avoid them killing you. Once the morning comes, all Animations will disappear until the next night.

Tips & Advice

As soon as you see the sun going down, immediately run to the closest house and hide. It's also a good idea to turn off the lights with a Light Switch so the Animations don't find you.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Hide in houses as soon as you see the sun going down.
  • Turn off the Lights in a house at night.
  • Move quickly through the Level.
  • If in a Truck, stay near the driver's seat to avoid being killed. This may not always work.
  • If playing with 3-4 players, split up with 2 players per house since there are only 3 hiding spots.

Don'ts

  • Stay outside after the sun goes down.
  • Stand around in the houses at night due to Animations being able to break into houses.
  • Stay near the exit door of a Truck.
  • Keep the Lights on in a house at night.
  • Go into the same house if you're playing with 3-4 players.
  • Run around in a house at night, the Animations will figure out you're in the house.

Trivia

  • While the actual time in Level 94 is unknown, Animations might spawn during 10 P.M. in the level considering the note in the level calling them the 10 P.M. Curfew.
  • Before Animations were created, Smilers were planned to spawn during the night in Level 94.
  • Animations come in multiple forms according to the Backrooms Wikidot, one of which being a form made of clay, but Fancy only made the wooden marionette variety in-game.
