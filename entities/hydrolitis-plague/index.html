Hydrolitis Plague

Minimal Bacteria-Like Plague

The Hydrolitis Plague is a bacteria-like plague that can be found in the Dark-Tiled Rooms in Level 37 and in some of the water in Level 37.2. It is extremely dangerous, so stay away from it, and use the Floatie Boat to get past it. Symptoms of the Hydrolitis Plague include pain and fever and there's no cure for it.

Survival card

DO

  • Stay in the Floatie Boat if it is in the water of the Expanded Poolrooms .
  • Avoid bodies of water that are infected with the Hydrolitis Plague, unless you have an item such as Juice .
  • If you fall into a body of water with it, leave the area as fast as possible.

DON'T

  • Enter it upon seeing it.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 37

Behavior

If you find the plague in water, upon entering you will slowly pass out and soon enough, you will die from it.

  • Enter it upon seeing it.

Trivia

  • This is the only non-sentient Entity in Escape the Backrooms.
