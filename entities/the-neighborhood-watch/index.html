Sighted in
Level 9

Appearance

The Neighborhood Watch is a giant walking eyeball with four legs and a brown eye. Instead of having feet, its feet are hands. The legs are green while the area around the eye is red/brown. It's legs and area around its eye has been stained with blood. Due to its immediate attempt to eliminate any Wanderer it sees, The Neighborhood Watch is a very dangerous Entity. The Neighborhood Watch is the exact reason why the streets are the most dangerous part about Level 9, so be careful when you're in the Level.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 9 - Darkened Suburbs

Behavior

The Neighborhood Watch is always patrolling the streets in a certain path. If it spots you, it will become faster than you and chase you down. If you want to escape it, you can hide inside of a House and wait for it to leave.

Tips & Advice

If The Neighborhood Watch kills someone while you stand right Infront of its legs, it will not be able to harm you anymore and instead follow you around Level 9. It will still be able to kill other players if lead near them.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Keep an eye out on the streets incase it's nearby.
  • Start running if you hear loud footsteps and look for a House.
  • Listen for its loud footsteps to decide where to hide.
  • Stay away from its line of sight (the front has more eyesight range than the backside).
  • Hide out of sight behind bushes or inside Houses to avoid being seen.

Don'ts

  • Run towards any loud footsteps you hear.
  • Get too close to it from behind.
  • Refrain from entering a House if it spots you.
  • Be in front of it.
  • Be in a cornered scenario with it and a Wretch.
  • Stand still on the streets.
  • Roam the streets fora long period of time on the roads it patrols.

Trivia

  • This Entity on the original Wikidot Page had different kinds of itself. There was only one kind that was done for Escape the Backrooms, which is a Strider.
  • The Neighborhood Watch was added after Jerry was removed.
  • Fancy talked about a way to get rid of The Neighborhood Watch which is by throwing it off its path once it gets to you, but this is most likely not possible.
  • The Neighborhood Watch has a specific set path that it takes and will repeat once it finishes.
  • It is possible to use The Neighborhood Watch to completely skip Level 9. First, you need to aggravate it, then go behind a tree and The Neighborhood Watch will glitch and totally forget about killing you. Then lure The Neighborhood Watch near the Administrator Card Terminal, jump into its eyeball and jump over the fence.
    • This method is used by many speedrunners to entirely beat Level 9 in under 1 minute.
