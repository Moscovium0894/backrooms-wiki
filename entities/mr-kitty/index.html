Appearance

Mr. Kitty appears to be a tall lanky humanoid figure with pointed limbs and black skin. He has 2 white eyes, wrinkled skin and lacks a mouth. He is the main entity that resides in Level 974.

Location

This entity can be found in:

  • Level 974

Behavior

After a few seconds of being in Level 974, Mr. Kitty will spawn somewhere nearby the player. If the player is looking at him, he won't move or attack if the player gets too close and every so often, he'll teleport to another location in the level. If the player is not looking at him, he will slowly move closer to the player and if he reaches you, he will kill you. He follows the player around the level waiting for Toys to be collected.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Keep your eyes on him.

Don'ts

  • Look away from him if he's nearby or right next to you.
  • Roam around Level 974 without knowing where he is for too long.
  • Carelessly lock yourself in a room thinking he won’t reach you, he can always spawn behind you to kill you and he can open doors.

Trivia

  • The current Mr. Kitty model hasn't always been the case. The original Mr. Kitty model was very different and appeared to be a lot more humanoid. Everybody ended up hating it because it didn't look like Mr. Kitty in the original Lore, so Fancy changed it to a more lore accurate model. The model was changed again when Fancy redesigned Level 974 and Mr. Kitty was changed to not look like an untextured stickman.
  • Fancy first made a joke Mr. Kitty model when he was making Mr. Kitty to show people that he's good at modeling Entities just to make a not so good model. Afterwards, he made the original Mr. Kitty model.
  • Mr. Kitty has the loudest jumpscare in the entire game.
  • In the backroom lore he's just referred to as Kitty but in-game he's called Mr. Kitty as confirmed by Fancy.
