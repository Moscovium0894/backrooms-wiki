Appearance

The Thing on Level 7 strangely enough has 2 different appearances, one of which matches an older Backrooms Fandom Wiki interpretation of The Thing, and the other matching the more commonly recognized appearance of The Thing.

Viper Fish

The Thing on Level 7 appears to be a average sized viper fish with a large row of sharp teeth, a blue eye, and 2 things extending out on its back. It is the only entity in Level 7 as it's believed to have killed every other form of life in the level.

Angler Fish

The Thing on Level 7's second appearance appears as a ginormous angler fish. This Thing doesn't attack, as it just shows up next to the Shark Tank when it's going to the bottom of the ocean in Level 7.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 7 - Thalassophobia

Behavior

Current Behavior

The Thing on Level 7 only attacks during the fog event, if a player is not in a Boat and nowhere near a house. After 15 seconds if these conditions are met, it jumps out of the water and eats the player, then goes back underwater.

Original Behavior

The Thing on Level 7 was originally completely different and had a model matching the current viper fish one but with less stuff on its body. The longer a player would spend in the Ocean, the more likely it would attack and if it chased you for long enough, it would start swimming towards you. In the ocean, it would have roamed around in search of players and if it was near you, you'd have to stand still or else it would eat you.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Stay in your Boat.
  • Stay close to your team unless you're playing alone so you don't have to swim to them.

Don'ts

  • Be in the water during the fog event.
  • Try to reach the edge of the surface of Level 7 during the fog event.

Trivia

  • The first Note in Level 7 has the appearance of The Original Thing on Level 7.
  • The Angler Fish Thing on Level 7 model is a free marketplace asset named SM_AnglerFish.
  • The reason Fancy added The Original Thing on Level 7 was because rowing in a Boat for hours on end would be pretty boring.
  • The original model of the Thing on Level 7 was quickly changed to the current one due to community feedback saying it should be larger.
