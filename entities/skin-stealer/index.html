Skin-Stealer

Deadly Humanoid

Skin-Stealers are humanoids with a mouth that goes down to their body. Its skin is gray, and it has 2 glowing white eyes. There are also various blood spots on it mainly around its mouth. Skin-Stealers possess the unique ability to steal a player's skin after killing them and disguise itself as a wanderer.

Appearance

Skin-Stealers are humanoids with a mouth that goes down to their body. Its skin is gray, and it has 2 glowing white eyes. There are also various blood spots on it mainly around its mouth. Skin-Stealers possess the unique ability to steal a player's skin after killing them and disguise itself as a wanderer.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 1 - Habitable Zone
  • Level 5 - Terror Hotel
  • Level 8 - Cave System
  • Level 0.2 (in Level 0.1)
  • Level 55.1 (in Level 0.11)

Behavior

Skin-Stealers will either start with skin on or without it. The Skin appears to be a hazmat suit from a former wanderer. At first glance, it is nearly impossible to spot the difference between a Player and a Disguised Skin-Stealer. Once a Skin-Stealer spots a Player, it will start chasing them and if they are disguised, once they get close enough the Skin will disappear, revealing their True Form. Skin-Stealers are slightly slower than the Player on most Levels except for Level 8 where they move at a significantly faster speed and will catch up easily to the Player that they are chasing. Skin-Stealers cannot open doors, so make sure to use that to your Advantage. Skin-Stealers cannot harm you if you are hiding under a Bed or in a Closet in Level 5, even if it saw you hide, although it's worth noting that the Skin-Stealer in Level 5 in particular has a 3 minute time after which it will constantly know your location, even if you are hiding and will not stop following you until it sees you, and in Level 8, they have Flashlights to help you spot them around corners or from a distance. You can also hide in Lockers in the Level 0.11 Layer in Level 55.1 and they won't be able to kill you.

Talking

Skin-Stealers can talk when close to make them seem normal. They can say 3 Things which include:

  • Uh, hello?
  • Excuse me?
  • Hey, hey!

It is to get players attention and to lure them into thinking they are another player. Do not be fooled.

You can detect a Skin-Stealer by the talking they do. They also stand out and have glowing eyes when not disguised, making it easy to see them from long distances and in the dark. If you see a small white dot high up that is moving, that is a Skin-Stealer. Additionally, hostile entities won't attack a Skin-Stealer. If you find out that a hostile entity sees a player and doesn't attack them, that player is a Skin-Stealer. Alternatively, they carry Flashlights which alert you to their location on Level 8.

Tips & Advice

If you're playing Multiplayer, make sure to stick together. In case you do get separated, make sure everyone will always pull out their ID Card when they see another player. If someone doesn't do the same and starts running straight at you, it's a Skin-Stealer, so run away and go to the nearest Door. Teamwork and communication are the best ways to survive a Skin-Stealer.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Run away from any Players that appear to be chasing you.
  • Shut the doors behind you to lose the Skin-Stealer's aggression.
  • Keep communication with your team in Multiplayer to avoid being tricked.
  • Peek around corners to avoid running into one on accident.

Level 1

  • Hide behind Hiding Doors on Floor 3 in Level 1.

Level 5

  • Hide in the Closets or under the Beds in the Hotel Rooms in The Hotel or close the Door.

Level 8

  • Running back to the start or the Exit and turning the Valve to shut the transporter Door is ideal, though if you manage to lose line of sight for long enough, it will stop chasing you (Does not happen as often due to the Skin-Stealers being faster than the Player in Level 8).

Level 0.1

  • Run into a Generator room and close the Door or run to the nearest Noclipping Floor and jump into it to escape the Skin-Stealer.

Level 55.1

  • Hide inside a Locker or use the Elevator and go up out of reach to avoid it getting to you.

Don'ts

  • Attempt to ask someone running at you to see their ID Card, they won't stop.
  • Leave Doors behind you open.
  • Attempt to Fight the Skin-Stealer.
  • Spam F on a Door when being chased down by a Skin-Stealer, stay calm and open the Door once before closing it once.
  • Fall for their Talking.
  • Press F on a door with another player at the same time while it's chasing you, stay calm and decides who opens the door and closes it
