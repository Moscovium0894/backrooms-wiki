Party Host

Indirectly Deadly Cake with Eyeball

Party Hosts are cakes with bright green icing with an Eyeball in the middle. The color of the eye is blue. Party Hosts are immobile and will always be on the wall or the ceiling.

Survival card

DO

  • Throw a Firework directly at them from a safe distance to kill them and not kill yourself from the blast.
  • Kill them so you can proceed through the Funhouse Sector.
  • Take note of the closest Table to hide under when one alerts the Partygoers of your location.

DON'T

  • Stand still when spotted, they will always alert the Partygoers .
  • Throw a Firework when too close to them.
  • Stand out in the open with no intent of hiding or killing one, Partygoers will come after you.
Sighted in
Level Fun Level Fun+

Appearance

Party Hosts are cakes with bright green icing with an Eyeball in the middle. The color of the eye is blue. Party Hosts are immobile and will always be on the wall or the ceiling.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level Fun+

Behavior

Party Hosts can be found throughout the Funhouse Sector and once they spot you, the Partygoers will leave their rooms and start hunting the player. Simply hide underneath a Table and they will go back to their rooms. Throw a Firework at a Party Host to kill it.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Throw a Firework directly at them from a safe distance to kill them and not kill yourself from the blast.
  • Kill them so you can proceed through the Funhouse Sector.
  • Take note of the closest Table to hide under when one alerts the Partygoers of your location.

Don'ts

  • Stand still when spotted, they will always alert the Partygoers.
  • Throw a Firework when too close to them.
  • Stand out in the open with no intent of hiding or killing one, Partygoers will come after you.

Trivia

  • Party Hosts have the power to control the entirety of Level Fun+, which is mentioned in a Note.
  • Because Level Fun+ is a fanmade Backrooms level, Party Hosts live in Level Fun in the original Backrooms lore.
