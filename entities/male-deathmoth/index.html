Male Deathmoth

Friendly Moths

Male Deathmoths are a much smaller species of greyish moths. The Male Deathmoths do not attack the Player.

Sighted in
Level 5 Level 9

Appearance

Male Deathmoths are a much smaller species of greyish moths. The Male Deathmoths do not attack the Player.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 5
  • Level 9

Behavior

Male Deathmoths fly around in one place in groups scattered around Level 5 and parts of Level 9. If you find them, you can spray Bug Spray on them to kill them, and they will drop Moth Jelly.

Tips & Advice

Since Male Deathmoths are harmless, they are easy to kill. The Moth Jelly is used to access The Hotel in Level 5. Moth Jelly is a pretty useful Item for Sanity and Speed. When you enter The Main Halls, 4 Male Deathmoths will be in selected locations. Every time you kill a group of them (except for the first), a new Male Deathmoth will appear in a different selected location, so after killing one Male Deathmoth, only 3 will ever be in The Main Hall at once.

Trivia

  • Male Deathmoths are the first Friendly Entity in the Game.
  • Fancy showed a different design when he made the Male Deathmoths, which resembles the Female Deathmoths but much smaller.
    • The Male Deathmoths seen in-game are smaller groups of Male Deathmoths.
  • Despite being Moths, Male Deathmoths make housefly noises instead of Moth noises. This might be because Moths don't make noise aside from their wings flapping.
  • There are Male Deathmoths that fly around just under the streetlights in Level 9 that appear a bit too high to reach by a player to get Moth Jelly from. However, they can be reached if the player stands on the rim of a bench if applicable and jumps repeatedly to reach the moths while using Bug Spray, they can successfully kill them and get the Jelly upon it falling to the ground.)
