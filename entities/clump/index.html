Clump

Dangerous Creature with a large amount of arms

Clumps are large, fleshy beasts that appear to have tons of human arms on their bodies. They have a torso solely made out of their mouth, and a tail on the back of them. Their mouths have round teeth and a large tongue, and they devour their victims upon finding them.

Survival card

  • Run away from it in the red hallways.
  • Stay in the blue hallways whenever you get the chance, the Clump can't enter them.

DON'T

Sighted in
Level !~! Level 37.2

Appearance

Clumps are large, fleshy beasts that appear to have tons of human arms on their bodies. They have a torso solely made out of their mouth, and a tail on the back of them. Their mouths have round teeth and a large tongue, and they devour their victims upon finding them. Clumps are very fast creatures and can easily catch up to a Wanderer.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level !~! - Hotel Chase
  • Level 37.2 - Dark Poolrooms

Behavior

Level !~!

In Level !~!, the Clump that inhabits the level will follow the closest player to it, even if they wander off course. It cannot enter Blue Hallways.

Level 37.2

In all 3 of the Level 37.2s, a Clump will roam around the level aimlessly. If it spots a player, it will chase them, however if they enter deep water, it will walk away and continue to roam around the level.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

Level !~!

  • Run away from it in the red hallways.
  • Stay in the blue hallways whenever you get the chance, the Clump can't enter them.
  • Drink Juice or eat some Moth Jelly for a speed boost to increase your distance away from it.
  • Don't look back to see it since you will slow down or even stop moving for a moment.

Level 37.2

  • Look for it before entering a room.
  • Enter nearby deep water if it spots you.
  • Keep out of sight.
  • Peek around corners with Q and E before proceeding forward.
  • Jump into a body of water so it stops chasing you.
  • Keep note of where it is before turning any Valves since it makes no noise at all except for when it kills a player.

Don'ts

Level !~!

  • Enter the red hallways when you see blue ones.
  • Walk through the level instead of run.

Level 37.2

  • Enter a room without checking it first.
  • Avoid the water if the Clump spots you.
  • Run around a corner blindly.
  • Start turning a Valve if you don't know where it is (this opens you up for a surprise attack).

Trivia

  • Clumps seem to use mainly their lower arms to move, despite there being some that they could use to have more arms moving at once which would make it even faster.
  • In Level !~!, the Clump will lose the player it's chasing if they jump onto furniture in the hotel lobby room.
  • Because Clumps don't have eyes, it doesn't make sense how they rely on sight to find players in Level 37.2.
  • The Clump in Level !~! makes a bit of sense with the original Backrooms lore because in it they are mentioned to appear in levels with tighter spaces, and in Level !~!, the Clump there takes up a lot of space in the hallways.
  • This Entity was modeled by Brian V.H.
  • In the original Backrooms lore, Clumps are a bit different to how they are in-game and are more like an actual clump because they are clumps of human limbs instead of the limbs being attached to their body.
  • In the original Backrooms lore, Clumps are much more common entities that are mentioned to appear in majority of the levels.
  • Fancy considered adding a fully dynamic tentacle system for the Entity that could wrap around players instead of using a model but just ended up having Brian model it instead since it did not end up how he thought it would.
