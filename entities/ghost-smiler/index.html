Description

The Ghost Smiler is an entity only appearing in Level 1. It does not possess any physical form, only appearing in an event which results in the player's immediate death. During that appearance it resembles a regular smiler.

Level 1

The Ghost Smiler would appear when the player would try heading to the second floor of level 1 (as seen in the gallery) resulting in the player's death with a strange, zooming in jumpscare.

Additional information

The Ghost Smiler can be seen in multiple of Fancy's video thumbnails as well as official art. The Ghost Smiler was only in the game in Initial Release/Part 1 and was removed in the Part 2 update.
