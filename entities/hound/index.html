Appearance

Hounds are Skinny Humanoid Dogs with medium length black hair going down on both sides of their head and black eyes with white pupils.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 3
  • Level 5

Behavior

Hounds will walk around randomly. If a Hound spots you, it will start chasing you. Hounds are also faster than the Player. In order to get rid of it, shine a Flashlight in its eyes and it will be blinded for a few seconds. Then, it will start chasing you again. You must shine the Light in the Hounds Eyes 3 times to get them back to roaming where they started. Hounds can't open doors, so you can use the doors to get away from Hounds. After shining certain Hounds 3 times, they will disappear. In Easy and Normal, you can shine a Hound multiple times, with just over a second between shines. In Hard and Nightmare, Hounds can only be stunned once. This will reset once they stop chasing you.

Tips & Advice

Always make sure to have a Flashlight in Zone 2 and 3 in Level 3 and in the Hotel in Level 5. If you're in the Hotel in Level 5, depending on the Difficulty, after stunning the Hound 3 times or losing it after it chases you it will despawn.

Do's & Don'ts

Do’s

  • Use a Flashlight to blind the Hounds.
  • Shut a door if a Hound sees you.
  • Be as quick as possible to react if a Hound is near you.
  • Carry a Flashlight.

Don'ts

  • Try to outrun the Hounds (this can work sometimes, but not always so don't take the risk if you're not that good)
  • Try to jump over the Hounds.
  • Attempt to crouch around the Hound.
  • Risk getting in between multiple Hounds.
