Wretch

Wretches are past wanderers that have gone insane due to loss of Sanity . Wretches still have their human shape, but they have no skin, leading for their outer layer to be bright red and exposing their flesh and tissue.

Appearance

Wretches are past wanderers that have gone insane due to loss of Sanity. Wretches still have their human shape, but they have no skin, leading for their outer layer to be bright red and exposing their flesh and tissue.

Location

This Entity can be found in:

  • Level 6 - Lights Out
  • Level 9 - Darkened Suburbs
  • Abandoned Outpost

Behavior

Level 6

When exploring the Flashlight Area in Level 6, shortly in, you will spot a Wretch walking through a hallway. It will not harm you, and it is possible to see it reach the end of the hallway in which it disappears. Once you reach the Maze, there will be a Wretch roaming around. Luckily, LiDAR Scanners have a built-in feature that Tracks the Wretch. Pick up a LiDAR Scanner, and you will see a screen on it. If there is a Red Dot, that is the Wretch. The Dot will get closer to the center as it gets closer to you, and the noise will get louder. If the Wretch is close, you will hear its breathing. In order to avoid it finding you, you must hide on the sides of the Maze, and it won't be able to find you. Due to the darkness of Level 6, the Wretch cannot see you.

Level 9

In Level 9, Wretches live in 6 different Houses and they hold Crowbars. If one sees you, it will start to chase you. They do not breath in Level 9, meaning you will have to listen for their footsteps. In order to hide from it, you must hide in a room and close the door. Wretches know how to open doors, so you will have to lock the door and they won't be able to get in.

Abandoned Outpost

In Abandoned Outpost, there is a Wretch roaming around. It has an Administrator Card and a Crowbar, and you have to collect the Card to escape Level 9. Unlike Level 9, It breathes in Abandoned Outpost. There are Lockers scattered throughout the Outpost, and if the Wretch spots you, hide in a Locker and it will go away. You can also hide in the Vent, since it cannot enter the Vent.

Tips & Advice

Level 6

Do's

  • Crouch whenever you hear a Wretch coming close to you using the LiDAR Scanner.
  • Use a LiDAR Scanner to know when a Wretch is close.

Don'ts

  • Walk or Run when a Wretch is nearby.
  • Freak out when the Wretch is bumping into you.
  • Stay in the path of the Wretch.

Level 9

Do's

  • Close the doors on them. Make sure to lock the door because Wretches are able to open doors.
  • Run out of the House that one's in.

Don'ts

  • Forget to lock the doors.
  • Crouch when near them, they can see you unlike Level 6.

Abandoned Outpost

Do's

  • Hide in a Locker or in the Vent to avoid being seen.
  • Crouch so it doesn't hear you.
  • Listen for doors opening, it might be passing through one nearby.

Don'ts

  • Run around blindly into rooms or around corners.
  • Try and trap it early, get all of the Almond Concentrate in the container first.

Trivia

  • The Wretch has 2 different Jumpscares: one in Level 6 and one for Level 9 and Abandoned Outpost.
  • There are multiple Sounds the Wretch can make in the Level 9 and Abandoned Outpost Jumpscare.
